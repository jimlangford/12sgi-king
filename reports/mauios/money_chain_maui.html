One joined view of Maui’s public money, up and down the chain: who funds (federal / state / county), who they pay (the prime — a nonprofit or a vendor), and who the prime pays underneath (the subcontractor). Where one name turns up in more than one layer — a 990 nonprofit that is also a federal subrecipient, a county vendor that is also a campaign donor — we surface it as a question to verify, never a finding.
