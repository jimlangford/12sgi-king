Under tonight's Kāloakūkahi, an organization’s people giving together is lawful and ordinary — the question is only whether a later decision answers the public or the people who funded the seat. The official can disclose, recuse, or decide in the open; the organization can say plainly what it seeks. Aloha is the ask.
