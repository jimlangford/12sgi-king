Title 19 Crosswalk — Charter Art. XI ↔ Maui County Zoning

The 12 Stones Sovereign Charter Article XI (Land Use & Zoning) states the principle; Maui County Code Title 19 (Zoning) is the existing, enforceable law that already implements much of it. This crosswalk maps the principle to the statute — so the public can see the law already on the books.

Maui County Code Title 19 is organized into Article I — Interim Zoning Provisions and Article II — Comprehensive Zoning Provisions. A full clause-by-clause mapping requires the code text (linked below); this maps the chapter structure and the governing mechanisms. Title 19 is currently being rewritten (the county’s Title 19 Rewrite project) — verify against the live code before relying on any citation.

How a vote becomes land use (and where to watch)

A Title 19 amendment or rezoning runs through the Council’s Housing & Land Use committee. Kilo Aupuni tracks each one: the agenda (council-watch), the vote & any recusals (votes-watch), and the money behind the officials (donor-watch). A zoning change next to developer money is a question to verify, not a finding.

Integrity: this page maps public law to a public principle. It alleges nothing about any person. Where a specific rezoning raises questions, those live in the Council & Patterns dashboards as facts + sources.
