## Key definitions (§15-2-5, verbatim)
- "Board" = the Board of Variances and Appeals OR the Board of Code Appeals, whichever is applicable.
- "Continuing violation" = any violation which continues beyond the time for correction set in the Director's order.
- "Repeat violation" = a subsequent violation causing a NOV/Order/Stop Work Order for violation of the same section of law on the same property within a one-year period from the date of the previous violation.
- "Violation" = (1) use/location/construction of structure or land without a required permit/approval; (2) use/location/construction not permitted by law or by permit conditions; (3) misrepresentation of fact on any application/plan/submittal.

## §15-2-6 Issuance of order (verbatim summary)
NOV+Order served in person or by certified mail (restricted delivery, return receipt). Order states date of notice, name/address of violator, provision violated, brief description, location (TMK or street address), date of violation. Order may require stop work / stop violation, correct before a date, pay a fine before a date, and pay an additional fine for each day the violation continues. Order becomes **final 30 days** after mailing/delivery unless an appeal is filed with the Board; appeal must be received within 30 days.

## §15-2-8 Time period for compliance — Schedule for Correction (verbatim)
Development Standard (setback, height, lot size/width, floor area, lot coverage): 30 days · Permit Conditions: 30 days · Building w/o permit: 30 days · Occupying w/o certificate of occupancy: 30 days · Demolition w/o permit: 30 days · Electrical/plumbing w/o permit: 7 days · Relocation of building w/o permit: 30 days · Not complying with stop work order: Immediately · Change in use w/o permit/CO: 7 days · Failure to follow approved plans: 30 days · Safety hazards: Immediately · Unsafe buildings: 30 days · Building-code violation with permit — Sign: 7 days, Use: Immediately · Other: as determined by the Director.

## §15-2-9 Administrative fines — Schedule of Civil Monetary Fines (verbatim: initial / daily)
- Development Standard — $1,000 / $100
- Misrepresentations — $1,000 / $100
- Permit Conditions — $1,000 / $100
- Building w/o a building permit — $200 / $50
- Occupying a building w/o a certificate of occupancy — $50 / $50
- Demolition w/o a building permit — $50 / $50
- Electrical and/or plumbing work w/o a permit — $200 / $200
- Relocation of a building w/o a permit — $50 / $50
- Not complying with stop work order — $1,000 / $100
- Change in use of building/space w/o permit/CO — $50 / $50
- Construction not following approved plans — $200 / $50
- Hazardous occupancies (fire alarm, sprinkler, standpipe, pedestrian protection, safety glazing, barb wire, pool enclosure, etc.) — $1,000 / $100
- Erecting temporary structures w/o a building permit (tents, booths) — $100 / $50
- Unsafe buildings — $1,000 / $100
- Building code violations with a building permit — $200 / $50
- Sign — $200 / $50
- Use — $1,000 / $100
Continuing violation: daily fine **doubles on the first day of each 30-day period** after the time to correct, **up to a maximum of $1,000/day**. Repeat violation: initial daily fine **doubled**. Daily fines may be waived on payment of the initial fine + written request (Director weighs degree, recurrence, cooperation, permit needs, corrective cost, etc.).

## §15-2-10/§15-2-11 (verbatim summary)
Joint and several liability among violators. Remedies are in addition to MCC remedies and do not preclude civil/criminal injunctive relief or criminal penalties.
