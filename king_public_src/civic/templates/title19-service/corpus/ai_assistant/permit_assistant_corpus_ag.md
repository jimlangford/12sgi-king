## Quick answers (each carries a cite)
- **Minimum lot size in AG?** 2 acres. [19.30A.030(A)]
- **Setbacks?** Front 25 ft; side & rear 15 ft. [19.30A.030(C)]
- **How many farm dwellings on Maui?** 2 per lot, one ≤1,500 sf developable area (Molokai: one ≤1,000 sf). [19.30A.050(B)(1)]
- **Farm labor dwelling?** 1 per 5 acres; on Maui meet 2 of 3 criteria — ≥$35,000 gross ag sales/yr for 2 yrs per dwelling; ag water-rate certification; or approved farm plan. [19.30A.050(B)(2)]
- **Is a B&B allowed in AG?** Yes, not on Molokai, if it meets the bona-fide-ag, small-lot/farm-plan, or historic-site criteria. [19.30A.050(B)(11)]
- **Short-term rental in AG?** Allowed under ch. 19.65 only with a fully implemented farm plan consistent with HRS ch. 205. [19.30A.050(B)(12)]
- **Is a golf course allowed?** No — prohibited. [19.30A.060(A)(7)]
- **Solar farm?** ≤15 acres & ≤35% of lot is a permitted use; >15 acres needs a special use permit. [19.30A.050(A)(8); 19.30A.060(A)(12)]
- **Agricultural tourism?** Permitted accessory use (not Molokai); register with Planning; 8am–6pm; must not interfere with farming. [19.30A.050(B)(15)]
- **Max developable area?** 10% of lot (applies to farm dwellings, not ag-support structures). [19.30A.030(D)]
- **What needs a special use permit?** Additional/non-qualifying farm or farm-labor dwellings, non-conforming commercial ag structures, major utilities, telecom antenna, cemeteries, churches, mining, landfills, solar >15 ac, certain open-land recreation. [19.30A.060(A)]

## Enforcement quick answers
- **Civil fine for a Title 19 violation?** Up to $1,000, or up to $1,000/day continuing; up to $20,000 initial / $10,000/day for unpermitted transient accommodations; or up to 1% of project cost. [19.530.030(B)(1)]
- **How do I contest a notice/order?** Appeal to the Board of Variances and Appeals within 30 days; the appeal does not stay the order. [19.530.030(B)(2),(C)]
- **Unpaid fines?** May be added to County taxes/fees (not residential water/sewer). [19.530.030(D)]
- **Criminal penalties?** Fine up to $1,000 + community service or imprisonment; each day can be a separate offense. [19.530.020]
