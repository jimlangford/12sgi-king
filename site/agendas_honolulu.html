Know what is being decided before it is decided. Upcoming public meetings as far ahead as this government posts them — so the community can show up, testify, and participate in time.

Source: HNLDoc (Honolulu Legislative Documents) — hosted at hnldoc.ehawaii.gov on the eHawaii.gov state portal platform (Hawaii Information Consortium / NIC-Hawaii subsidiary of Tyler Technologies). This is the REAL agenda/minutes document system for the Honolulu City Council — NOT Legistar, NOT CivicClerk, NOT Granicus-for-documents. Granicus (olelo.granicus.com) is used ONLY for meeting VIDEO, not agenda/document data. Human portal: https://honolulucitycouncil.org/ ↗  source verified
