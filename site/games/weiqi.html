Weiqi rules (5/5 verified)

Board: 9×9, intersections. Black plays first.

Place: Place a stone of your color on any empty intersection.

Capture: A stone (or group) with no liberties (empty adjacent intersections) is captured and removed.

Suicide rule: You may not place a stone that has no liberties after placement — unless doing so captures opponent stones first.

Ko: You may not recreate the immediately previous board position (simple ko). Superko enforced: no position may repeat at all.

Pass: A player may pass their turn. Two consecutive passes end the game.

Scoring: Chinese area scoring = stones on board + surrounded empty territory. Komi 7.5 added to White's score to compensate first-mover advantage.
