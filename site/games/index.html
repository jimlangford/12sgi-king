Shared SAGE canon · the through-line

SAGE ↔ Mahjong

54 nodes = 108 suit tiles ÷ 2 (Bamboo · Circles · Characters × 36 each)
4 lineages = 4 winds (East · South · West · North)
Mauka lineage → East wind (morning, rising)
Kula lineage → South wind (noon, heat)
Makai lineage → West wind (evening, ocean)
Universal lineage → North wind (night, stars)

SAGE ↔ Konane

54 nodes span Ao · Pō phases — same duality as black / white ʻiliʻili
Capture move = removing a Hewa (broken-pair) node
Papamū board = the ahupuaʻa grid: Mauka top, Makai bottom
Win condition = opponent cannot move = pono restored

SAGE ↔ Weiqi

Liberties = open governance pathways; surround = policy capture
Komi 7.5 = acknowledgment that Black (the opener) holds first-mover advantage
Superko = no repeating a board position — same as no repeating a failed policy
Territory + stones = Chinese area scoring mirrors civic land + people

Kumulipo Ao · Pō cycle

Ao (day) — choices made: game play + civic action
Pō (night) — HINA uses the creative system to balance the equation those choices created
13 moons = 13 rounds of SAGE = one kaulana mahina year
54 nodes = one full SAGE deck = one full Mahjong suit half
