Agendas — Hong Kong SAR

Know what is being decided before it is decided. Upcoming public meetings as far ahead as this government posts them — so the community can show up, testify, and participate in time.

Source: LegCo (Legislative Council of the Hong Kong SAR) official website — legco.gov.hk. Council meeting agendas, panel/committee meeting schedules and agenda documents are published per-meeting (e.g. counmtg/agenda/cmYYYYMMDD.htm) and aggregated in the LegCo Calendar.
