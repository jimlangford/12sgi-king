This is all the money behind Hawaiʻi County — $1,378,755 in reported contributions — set beside its decisions. Read it as a question, never an accusation. Real estate is the slice we have traced deepest (donor → parcel → recorded sale), but it is only 3% of the giving; the larger money is contractors, tourism & hotels, unions, and others — those traces deepen as the records open. Where any interest funds the seat and holds business before it, that is the pair to watch.

The real-estate slice — the deepest-traced loop

$42,035 · 3% of giving
Real-estate interests gave $42,035 to this council and also build and sell land here. Hawaiʻi County contract awards are not yet in the open-data feed, and its sales extract is being sourced. The donor side is real and shown; the loop completes as the county's records open.
☾☀ Curse-breaker. Under tonight's Hilo, the path to pono is the same on every island: the official can disclose real-estate giving before a land-use or budget vote, recuse where the interest is direct, or decide in the open; the interest can say plainly what it seeks.
