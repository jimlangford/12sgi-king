Studio identity

One studio shell. Several protected systems.

Element Lotus is the public-facing studio shell for 12 Stones Global. TribeGameStudios, the film slate, and the music lane now sit at the front, while govOS and owner-only tools stay in their own lanes with clearer boundaries.

What changed

Public

Studio-first front door

Homepage and supporting pages now foreground games, films, and music instead of leading with civic dashboards.

Private

Owner systems stay protected

Tailscale, workboard, review queues, render control, and other owner-only surfaces remain outside the public shell.

Bridge

Interactive artifacts stay static

Playable games remain on the GitHub Pages/static bridge so WordPress can focus on brand, narrative, and conversion.

Canon

The studio worldview is still rooted in the SAGE canon: 54 nodes, 4 lineages, 4 winds, and the Ao · Pō rhythm. That shared structure gives continuity across games, films, and music without forcing civic material into every front-page moment.
