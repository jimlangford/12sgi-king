☾☀ Curse-breaker. Showing up to testify is the most ordinary, honorable civic act there is — a neighbor speaking to their government in the open. The only question this page asks is the narrow one: when a testifier also funds the seat or holds an interest in the matter, the decider can disclose and recuse, and the testifier can say plainly whom they speak for. Named in aloha, under tonight’s Kāloakūkahi — the record simply made legible, never an accusation.
