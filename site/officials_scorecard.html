Maui County Officials — Votes, Recusals & the Money

Built only from public meeting minutes. Recusals are formal conflict-of-interest declarations — the cleanest paper trail of where an official has a financial or relational stake.  ·  See the nay narratives — every motion where the council split, with the dissenter's own recorded words; and the money behind the seats.

These are documented facts and open questions, not findings of wrongdoing. A recusal is lawful and proper — it is also a roadmap of where to look. Every line links to the source minutes. Verify before you assert anything about any person.
