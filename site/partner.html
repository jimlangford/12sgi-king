Collaboration

Work that serves the same mission.

Element Lotus is a studio built around games, films, music, and civic transparency. Partnership conversations start from a shared sense of purpose — not a pitch deck. If the work serves people, preserves culture, or advances honest civic participation, we want to hear from you.

What we look for

Alignment with the mission: cultural honesty, civic transparency, and story-first creative work. Conversations that stay proportionate — you won't need to know our internal systems, and we won't need yours. The public work stands on its own.

How to start

Use the WordPress contact and join flow for partnership inquiries. Keep it brief and direct: what the work is, who it serves, and why the two studios or organizations belong in the same conversation.
