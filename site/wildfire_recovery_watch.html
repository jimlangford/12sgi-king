Built from public Notices of Award (HANDS, hands.ehawaii.gov). Winning recovery work is lawful and necessary — rebuilding Lahaina takes contractors. This is the map of who is paid, so it can be read beside who funds the deciders. Documented facts and open questions, not findings of wrongdoing.
The open question. The recovery money is concentrated — 53% of $22,628,722 flows to just three firms, and 7 firms hold repeat awards. In the current public records there is no campaign-donation link found between these recovery awardees and the deciding officials. The connections still to verify — the records to demand next — are subcontractor chains on the prime contracts, sole-source / emergency-procurement justifications used post-disaster, lobbyist registrations, and official recusals. That is where capture, if any, would hide.
