Liverpool & Onondaga — The Pattern: Contracts × Donors

Do county vendors donate to the officials who decide county contracts? This page cross-references Onondaga County contract awards (data.ny.gov rb9h-9fit, $150M, 794 awards, 67 vendors) with Liverpool-area campaign contributions (NYS Board of Elections, $3.8M since 1999) to surface the pattern — framed as questions, never findings of wrongdoing. Pattern overview

How to read this
The Onondaga County contract dataset covers $150.5M in awards across 67 distinct vendors from the county procurement system. Liverpool-area political giving spans $3.8M to 1,236 recipient committees. Overlap between these two sets — vendors who also appear as registered contributors — is the pattern this page tracks. The full automated join awaits the dedicated fetcher pipeline; this page establishes the civic framing and source record so the dimension is live.

Prosecutorial lens (JRCSL protocol)

Per the JRCSL prosecutorial lens this is NOT an allegation — it is a civic question the sourced record can answer.
