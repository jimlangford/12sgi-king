New York City — Who Funds the Officials

Every campaign contribution reported to the NYC Campaign Finance Board, aggregated by who received it. This is the money side of the map: which candidates and officials draw the most public donor money, and where that money comes from. Self-funding and large loans (single contributions of $250,000+) are separated out so the donor picture is not distorted by a billionaire writing their own campaign a check.
