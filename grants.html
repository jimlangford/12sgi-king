How the methodology works
01
Match
Your project is compared against 16 active programs. The right program is determined by project type, jurisdiction, and eligibility — not just keyword matching.
02
Frame
Every funder has a funding lens — LMI benefit, benefit-cost ratio, unmet need, value-added product. We match your project's actual outcomes to the lens the reviewer uses to score.
03
Source
Applications are grounded in real public data — census tracts, FEMA loss history, HANDS contract awards, USDA farm data — not generic boilerplate.
04
Draft
AI-assisted drafting pulls from 97 authored applications to produce a narrative that matches the program's voice, score sheet, and required sections.
05
Review
You review the draft. We refine. The final submission stays under your organization's name and signing authority. You own the work product.
06
Submit
Grants.gov, Grants.hawaii.gov, or the funder's portal — we walk you through submission. One click, one PDF, deadline tracked.
