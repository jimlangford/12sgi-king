NOTICE AND AGENDA
The Council and its Budget & Finance Committee will follow the ensuing
schedule in reviewing, deliberating upon, and making preliminary decisions
concerning the Mayor’s Annual Budget for Fiscal Year 2015-2016. The Mayor’s
Annual Budget consists of Bill No. 2582, “A Bill For An Ordinance Relating To The
Operating Budget And Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2015 to
June 30, 2016;” Bill No. 2583, “A Bill For An Ordinance Relating To Capital
Improvements And Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2015 to
June 30, 2016;” and Resolution No. 2015-26, “Resolution Establishing The Real
Property Tax Rates For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2015 to June 30, 2016 For The
County of Kaua’i” (hereafter collectively called the “Mayor’s 2015-2016 Annual
Budget”).
Unless otherwise noted, all of the following meetings concerning the
Mayor’s 2015-2016 Annual Budget will be held at 4396 Rice Street, Suite 201,
Lihu’e, at 9:00 a.m. or as soon as possible thereafter.
Public Testimony during Departmental Budget Reviews will be taken at the
beginning of each day, at approximately 9:00 a.m. or soon thereafter.

For Bill No. 2582, “A Bill For An Ordinance Relating To The Operating Budget and
Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2015 To June 30, 2016,” Deliberation and
Preliminary Decision-Making is for purposes of Council discussion, deliberation, and
preliminary decision-making regarding various positions, specific budget revenue or
appropriation items, or both, and various general budget provisos.
For Bill No. 2583, “A Bill For An Ordinance Relating To Capital Improvements And
Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2015 To June 30, 2016,” Deliberation and
Preliminary Decision-Making is for purposes of Council discussion, deliberation, and
preliminary decision-making regarding various revenue or appropriation items, or both,
and various general budget provisos.
For Resolution No. 20 15-26, “Resolution Establishing The Real Property Tax Rates
For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2015 To June 30, 2016 For The County Of Kaua’i,” Deliberation
and Preliminary Decision-Making is for purposes of Council discussion, deliberation, and
preliminary decision-making regarding the various tax rates described in this resolution.
The Budget & Finance Committee may also consider the following in its
Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making on the Mayor’s 2015-2016 Annual Budget:
1. Mayor’s May 8, 2015 Budget Modification Communication to the Council;
2. Items discussed in Departmental Budget Reviews held between
March 27 — April 16, 2015;
3. Items discussed in Departmental Call-Backs and Additional Departmental
Budget Reviews held between April 17 — April 24, 2015; and
4. Items relating to the budget, including, but not limited to, use of a line-item or
program budget format.
For all of the Mayor’s 2015-2016 Annual Budget, Deliberation, and Preliminary
Decision-Making is also for purposes of facilitating the making of amendments to
Bill No. 2582, Bill No. 2583, and Resolution No. 20 15-26 at the Council’s subsequent
meeting of the Budget & Finance Committee regarding these items.
Each Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making meeting scheduled between
May 14, 2015 and May 18, 2015 is subject to cancellation by the Chairperson of the
Council’s Budget & Finance Committee.
Meeting dates listed after May 14, 2015 are a continuation of the May 14, 2015
meeting. For each meeting date after May 14, 2015, interested persons should refer to
individual meeting notices posted for each such meeting for the current status of each of
these meetings.
Public Testimony during Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making will be
taken at the beginning of each day, at approximately 9:00 a.m. or soon thereafter.
