KAUA'I COUNTY COUNCIL - NOTICE AND AGENDA 
The schedule below relates to the review, deliberation, and decision-making of 
the Council and its Committee of the Whole concerning the Mayor's Annual Budget 
for Fiscal Year 2024-2025, which collectively consists of Bill No. 2916, "A Bill For An 
Ordinance Relating To The Operating Budget And Financing Thereof For The Fiscal 
Year July 1, 2024 to June 30, 2025"; Bill No. 2917, "A Bill For An Ordinance Relating 
To Capital Improvements And Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2024 to 
June 30, 2025"; and Resolution No. 2024-13, "Resolution Establishing The Real 
Property Tax Rates For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2024 to June 30, 2025 For The County 
of Kaua'i." 
DELIBERATION AND PRELIMINARY DECISION-MAKING 
BY THE COUNCIL'S COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE 
ON THE MAYOR'S 2024-2025 ANNUAL BUDGET 
WILL BE HELD, AS NECESSARY, ON THE FOLLOWING DATES AND TIMES: 
Tuesday, May 14, 2024 
Wednesday, May 15, 2024 
9:00 a.m. - 4:30 p.m. 
(as appropriate and determined by the Chair of the Council's Committee of the Whole) 
The purpose of Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making is the 
deliberation and preliminary decision-making regarding various positions, specific 
budget revenue or appropriation items, budget provisos, and real property tax rates, 
which could result in amendments to Bill No. 2916, Bill No. 2917, and Resolution 
No. 2024-13. The Committee of the ·whole may also consider the following in its 
Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making on the Mayor's 2024-2025 Annual 
Budget: (1) the Mayor's Supplemental Budget Modification Communication to the 
Council; (2) items discussed in Departmental Budget Reviews; (3) items discussed in 
Departmental Call-Backs; and (4) items relating to the budget, including, but not 
limited to, use of a line-item or program budget format. 
Meeting dates listed after May 14, 2024 are a continuation of the May 14, 2024 
meeting. Meetings are subject to cancelation. Interested persons may contact the 
Council Services Division to determine the status of any meeting. 
MEETING INFORMATION: 
• In-Person Attendance: Unless otherwise noted, all meetings concerning the 
Mayor's 2024-2025 Annual Budget will be held at the Council Chambers, Historic 
County Building, 4396 Rice Street, Suite 201, Lihu'e, Hawai'i, 96766, at 9:00 a.m. 
or soon thereafter. 
• Oral Testimony: Accepted in-person at the Council Chambers. 
• Written Testimony: May be submitted for any meeting via: email to 
counciltestimon y@kauai. gov; fax to (808) 241-6349; mail to the Office of the 
County Clerk, Council Services Division at 4396 Rice Street, Suite 209, Lihu'e, 
Hawai'i, 96766; or hand-delivery at Suite 209 during regular business 
hours (7:45 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday, excluding holidays). 
BUDGET DELIBERATION AND PRELIMINARY DECISION-MAKING 2 
• Courtesy Livestream, Video, and Minutes: Meeting livestream (recorded video 
to follow) is available at www.kauai.gov/Government/Council/Webcast-Meetings . 
If the courtesy live stream is unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed. 
Meeting minutes will be completed pursuant to Hawai'i Revised Statutes (HRS) 
Chapter 92 and electronically posted at www.kauai.gov/Government/Council 
following Council approval. 
• Courtesy Remote Oral Testimony: This is not a remote meeting pursuant to 
HRS Section 92-3. 7. The option of remote oral testimony is offered as a courtesy 
only. To register, testifiers shall sign up via emailing remote.signup @kauai.gov , 
indicating that they would like to provide testimony on the budget proceeding for 
the specified day. Emails are due no later than 12:00 Noon the business day prior 
to the meeting. Those who timely register will receive remote testimony access 
and instructions via reply email. Those wishing to view the meeting but not 
provide testimony will be directed to the courtesy livestream (via the website link 
above) . If the courtesy remote testimony access is unavailable for any reason, the 
meeting will proceed. 
• Additional Note for Both Methods of Oral Testimony: Generally, budget 
public testimony will be accepted at the beginning of each day (subject to change). 
Those seeking the guaranteed ability to testify should report: (1) in-person, 
and (2) by the meeting start time. 
• For Further Assistance: If you need an auxiliary aid/service, other 
accommodation due to a disability, or an interpreter for non-English speaking 
persons, please contact the Office of the County Clerk, Council Services Division 
at (808) 241-4188 or cokcouncil @kauai.gov as soon as possible. Requests made as 
early as possible will allow adequate time to fulfill your request. Upon request, 
this notice is available in alternate formats such as large print, braille, or 
electronic copy.
