MEETING INFORMATION:
This is an in-person meeting at multiple meeting sites connected by interactive conference
technology pursuant to Hawai‘i Revised Statutes (HRS) Section 92-3.5.  Councilmembers will
attend the meeting at the Council Chambers location only.  Members of the public may attend the
meeting at either location.
Oral Testimony:  
Accepted in-person at the Council Chambers and Meeting Room 106.
Written Testimony:
 May be submitted for any meeting via: email to
counciltestimony@kauai.gov
; fax to (808) 241-6349; mail to the Office of the County Clerk,
Council Services Division at 4396 Rice Street, Suite 209, Lihu‘e, Hawai‘i, 96766; or hand-
delivery at Suite 209 and Meeting Room 106 during regular business hours (7:45 a.m. to 4:30
p.m., Monday through Friday, excluding holidays).
Courtesy Livestream, Video, and Minutes:
 Meeting livestream (recorded video to follow) is
available at 
www.kauai.gov/Government/Council/Webcast-Meetings
.  If the courtesy livestream
is unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed.  Meeting minutes will be completed
pursuant to HRS Chapter 92 and electronically posted at 
www.kauai.gov/Government/Council
following Council approval.
Courtesy Remote Oral Testimony:
 This is 
not
 a remote meeting pursuant to HRS Section 92-3.7. 
The option of remote oral testimony is offered as a courtesy only.  To register, testifiers shall sign
up via emailing 
remote.signup@kauai.gov
, indicating the agenda item(s) they would like to
provide testimony on.  Emails are due no later than 12:00 Noon the business day prior to the
meeting.  Those who timely register will receive remote testimony access and instructions via
reply email.  Those wishing to view the meeting but not provide testimony will be directed to the
courtesy livestream (via the website link above).  If the courtesy remote testimony access is
unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed.
Additional Note for Both Methods of Oral Testimony:
 Generally, each agenda item is addressed
(and oral testimony received) in the order listed in the agenda; however, agenda items may occur
out of order without notice and oral testimony is not accepted for completed agenda items.  Those
seeking the guaranteed ability to testify should report: (1) in-person, and (2) by the meeting start
time.

ADDITIONAL NOTIFICATION – NO ELEVATOR ACCESS TO SECOND FLOOR:
At the time of this meeting notice and agenda posting, the Historic County Building elevator is
temporarily inoperable.  Because the Council Chambers is on the second floor of the building, an in-
person meeting site at Meeting Room 106 will be connected to the in-person meeting site at the Council
Chambers by interactive conference technology.  If audio communication to the meeting cannot be
maintained between the locations, the meeting shall be automatically recessed to restore
communication.  If the audio communication cannot be restored within thirty (30) minutes, the meeting
will be terminated.  If the audiovisual communication can be restored, the meeting may reconvene.  If
the audio communication can be restored, the meeting may reconvene with audio-only communication,
with hardcopies of visual aids made available to all meeting participants pursuant to HRS Section 92-
3.5(c).
