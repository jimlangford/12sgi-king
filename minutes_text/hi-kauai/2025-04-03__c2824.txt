MEETING INFORMATION: 
• In-Person Attendance: Unless otherwise noted, all meetings concerning the 
Mayor's Annual Budget will be held at the Council Chambers, Historic County 
Building, 4396 Rice Street, Suite 201, Lihu'e, Hawai'i, 96766, at 9:00 a.m. or soon 
thereafter. 
• Oral Testimony: Accepted in-person at the Council Chambers. 
• Written Testimony: May be submitted for any meeting via: email to 
counciltestimon v@kauai. gov; fax to (808) 241-6349; mail to the Office of the 
County Clerk, Council Services Division at 4396 Rice Street, Suite 209, Lihu'e, 
Hawai'i, 96766; or hand-delivery at Suite 209 during regular business 
hours (7:45 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday, excluding holidays). 
• Courtesy Live.stream, Video, and Minutes: Meeting livestream (recorded video 
to follow) is available at www.kauai. gov/Government/Council/Webcast-Meetin gs. 
If the courtesy livestream is unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed. 
Meeting minutes will be completed pursuant to Hawai'i Revised Statutes (HRS) 
Chapter 92 and electronically posted at www.kauai. gov/Government/Council 
following Council approval. 
• Courtesy Remote Oral Testimony: This is not a remote meeting pursuant to 
HRS Section 92-3. 7. The option of remote oral testimony is offered as a courtesy 
only. To register, testifiers shall sign up via emailing remote.si gnup@kauai. gov, 
indicating that they would like to provide testimony on the budget proceeding for 
the specified day. Emails are due no later than 12:00 Noon the business day prior 
to the meeting. Those who timely register will receive remote testimony access 
and instructions via reply email. Those wishing to view the meeting but not 
provide testimony will be directed to the courtesy livestream (via the website link 
above). If the courtesy remote testimony access is unavailable for any reason, the 
meeting will proceed. 
• Additional Note for Both Methods of Oral Testimony: Generally, budget 
public testimony will be accepted at the beginning of each day (subject to change). 
Those seeking the guaranteed ability to testify should report: (1) in-person, 
and (2) by the meeting start time. 
• For Further Assistance: If you need an auxiliary aid/service , other 
accommodation due to a disability, or an interpreter for non-English speaking 
persons, please contact the Office of the County Clerk , Council Services Division 
at (808) 241-4188 or cokcouncil @kauai. gov as soon as possible. Requests made as 
early as possible will allow adequate time to fulfill your request. Upon request, 
this notice is available in alternate formats such as large print, braille, or 
electronic copy.
