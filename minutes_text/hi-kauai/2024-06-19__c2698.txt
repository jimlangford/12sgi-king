MEETING INFORMATION: 
• Oral Testimony:  Accepted in-person at the Council Chambers. 
• Written Testimony:  May be submitted for any meeting via: email to 
counciltestimony@kauai.gov; fax to (808)  241-6349; mail to the Office of the 
County Clerk, Council Services Division at 4396 Rice Street, Suite 209, Līhu‘e, 
Hawai‘i, 96766; or hand -delivery at Suite 209 during regular business 
hours (7:45 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday, excluding holidays). 
• Courtesy Livestream, Video, and Minutes: Meeting livestream (recorded video 
to follow) is available at www.kauai.gov/Government/Council/Webcast-Meetings.  
If the courtesy livestream is unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed.  
Meeting minutes will be completed pursuant to Hawai‘i Revised Statutes (HRS) 
Chapter 92 and electronically posted at www.kauai.gov/Government/Council 
following Council approval. 
• Courtesy Remote Oral Testimony:  This is not a remote meeting pursuant to 
HRS Section 92-3.7.  The option of remote oral testimony is offered as a courtesy 
only.  To register, testifiers shall sign up via emailing remote.signup@kauai.gov, 
indicating the agenda item(s) they would like to provide testimony on.  Emails are 
due no later than 12:00 Noon the business day prior to the meeting.  Those who 
timely register will receive remote testimony access and instructions via reply 
email.  Those wishing to view the meeting but not provide testimony will be 
directed to the courtesy livestream (via the website link above ).  If the courtesy 
remote testimony access is unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed. 
• Additional Note for Both Methods of Oral Testimony: Generally, each agenda 
item is addressed (and oral testimony received) in the order listed in the agenda; 
however, agenda items may occur out of order without notice and oral testimony 
is not accepted for completed agenda items.  Those seeking the guaranteed ability 
to testify should report: (1) in-person, and (2) by the meeting start time. 
• For Further Assistance:  If you need an auxiliary aid/service, other 
accommodation due to a disability, or an interpreter for non -English speaking 
persons, please contact the Office of the County Clerk, Council Services Division 
at (808) 241-4188 or cokcouncil@kauai.gov as soon as possible.  Requests made as 
early as possible will allow adequate time to fulfill your request.  Upon request, 
this notice is available in alternate formats such as large print, braille, or 
electronic copy.
