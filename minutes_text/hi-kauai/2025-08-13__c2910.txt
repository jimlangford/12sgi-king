Courtesy Remote Oral Testimony:
 This is 
not
 a remote meeting pursuant to HRS Section 92-3.7.
The option of remote oral testimony is offered as a courtesy only. To register, testifiers shall sign
up via emailing 
remote.signup@kauai.gov
, indicating the agenda item(s) they would like to
provide testimony on. Emails are due no later than 12:00 Noon the business day prior to the
meeting. Those who timely register will receive remote testimony access and instructions via
reply email. Those wishing to view the meeting but not provide testimony will be directed to the
courtesy livestream (via the website link above). If the courtesy remote testimony access is
unavailable for any reason, the meeting will proceed.
Additional Note for Both Methods of Oral Testimony:
 Generally, each agenda item is addressed
(and oral testimony received) in the order listed in the agenda; however, agenda items may occur
out of order without notice and oral testimony is not accepted for completed agenda items. Those
seeking the guaranteed ability to testify should report: (1) in-person, and (2) by the meeting start
time.

C 2025-172
 
Communication (07/30/2025) from the Prosecuting Attorney, requesting Council
approval to receive and expend Opioid Settlement funds in the amount of $361,077.68, for
one (1) year, with the anticipated start date retroactive to December 15, 2024, and  approve
the indemnification provision in the Memorandum of Agreement between the State of
Hawai‘i and the County of Kaua‘i. The funds shall be used for establishing the Kaua‘i
Fentanyl Task Force to address opioid use disorders, mental health, and purposes of harm
reduction; supporting prevention education and deployment of naloxone, providing micro-
grants to community-based, faith-based, and public health community coalitions, and
substance use disorder/mental health providers, and supporting individuals in treatment and
their families.

implementation of the CZM Program on Kaua‘i commencing July 1, 2025 to June 30, 2026,
