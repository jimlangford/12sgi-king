Tuesday, May 14, 2019
For Bill No. 2738, “ABill For An Ordinance Relating To The Operating Budget and
Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2019 To June 30, 2020,” Deliberation and
Preliminary Decision-Making is for purposes of Council discussion, deliberation, and
preliminary decision-making regarding various positions, specific budget revenue or
appropriation items, or both, and various general budget provisos.
For Bill No. 2739, “ABill For An Ordinance Relating To Capital Improvements And
Financing Thereof For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2019 To June 30, 2020,” Deliberation and
Preliminary Decision-Making is for purposes of Council discussion, deliberation, and
preliminary decision-making regarding various revenue or appropriation items, or both,
and various general budget provisos.
For Resolution No. 2019-23, “Resolution Establishing The Real Property Tax Rates
For The Fiscal Year July 1, 2019 To June 30, 2020 For The County Of Kaua’i,”Deliberation
and Preliminary Decision-Making is for purposes of Council discussion, deliberation, and
preliminary decision-making regarding the various tax rates described in this Resolution.
The Committee of the Whole may also consider the following in its Deliberation and
Preliminary Decision-Making on the Mayor’s20 19-2020 Annual Budget:
1. Mayor’sMay 8, 2019 Supplemental Budget Modification Communication to the
Council;
2. Items discussed in Departmental Budget Reviews held between
March 28—April 15, 2019;
3. Items discussed in Departmental Call-Backs and Additional Departmental
Budget Reviews held between April 16 — April 26, 2019; and
4. Items relating to the budget, including, but not limited to, use of a line-item or
program budget format.
For all of the Mayor’s 20 19-2020 Annual Budget, Deliberation, and Preliminary
Decision-Making is also for purposes of facilitating the making of amendments to
Bill No. 2738, Bill No. 2739, and Resolution No. 20 19-23 at the Council’s subsequent
meeting of the Committee of the Whole regarding these items.
Each Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making meeting scheduled between
May 10, 2019 and May 14, 2019 is subject to cancellation by the Chairperson of the
Council’sCommittee of the Whole.
Meeting dates listed after May 10, 2019 are a continuation of the May 10, 2019
meeting. For each meeting date after May 10, 2019, interested persons should refer to
individual meeting notices posted for each such meeting for the current status of each of
these meetings.
Public Testimony during Deliberation and Preliminary Decision-Making will be
taken at the beginning of each day, at approximately 9:00 a.m. or soon thereafter.
NOTE: Interested persons should contact the Office of the County Clerk, Council
Services Division at (808) 241-4188 to determine the current status of any listed
Departmental Budget Review, Departmental Call-Back, or Deliberation and Preliminary
Decision-Making meeting.
