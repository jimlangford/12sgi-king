ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 137-25: REQUESTS THE DEPARTMENT OF WATER SUPPLY TO DEVELOP AND 
MAINTAIN AN ONLINE SYSTEM THAT DISPLAYS GENERAL WATER 
AVAILABILITY COUNTY-WIDE, INCLUDING NUMBER OF UNITS, 
ACTIVE COMMITMENTS, AND DEVELOPER AGREEMENTS  
Creates an online system hosted and maintained by the Department of Water 
Supply that would provide access to reliable, transparent, and up-to-date 
information about water availability for informed decision-making and to support 
the County’s long-term goals for sustainable developments and water resource 
management.  
 
Reference: Comm. 233 
Intr. by: Council Member Inaba
