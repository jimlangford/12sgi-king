BILLS FOR ORDINANCES  
 
Bill 194: AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLES 1, 2, 4, 5, AND 7, OF THE HAWAIʻI  
(2022-2024) COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO 
TELECOMMUNICATION ANTENNAS AND TOWERS     
 Amends the definition of “telecommunication antenna” and adds a definition for 
“telecommunication tower”, expands plan approval requirements, allows for 
telecommunication antennas or towers in any zoning district upon approval of the 
Planning Director, and revises siting, design, and operating regulations to align 
with State law. 
 
Reference: Comm. 985 
Intr. by: Council Member Evans 
Referred to Planning  
Commissions: August 23, 2024 
 
   ; and 
Comm. 985.50 From Managing Director William V. Brilhante, Jr., dated December 20, 2024, 
transmitting for Council’s consideration and action, letters from the  
Windward and Leeward Planning Commissions.

Bill 22: AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLE 5, OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY  
CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO HOSPITALS, 
SANITARIUMS, OLD AGE, CONVALESCENT, NURSING AND REST HOMES 
Adds hospitals, sanitariums, old age, convalescent, nursing and rest homes as 
permitted uses in the Industrial-Commercial Mixed Zoning District. 
 
Reference: Comm. 93 
Intr. by: Council Member Kierkiewicz (B/R)
