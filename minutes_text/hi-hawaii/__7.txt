BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 157:   AMENDS CHAPTER 7 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, 
AS AMENDED), RELATING TO DISASTER RECOVERY  
Establishes a County Disaster Recovery Program to coordinate short-and long-term 
recovery efforts following major disasters, led by a recovery officer appointed by the 
mayor with the responsibility for recovery planning, interagency coordination,  
community engagement, funding management, and the development of interim and  
long-term recovery and resilience plans. 
 
 Reference: Comm. 888 
 Intr. by: Council Member Kierkiewicz 
                                           Postponed:       May 19, 2026 
 
(Note:  There is a motion by Council Member Kierkiewicz, seconded by Council 
Member Hustace, to recommend passage of Bill 157 on first reading.)
