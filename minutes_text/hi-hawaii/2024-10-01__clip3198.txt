Oral Public Testimony via Zoom:  To provide oral testimony via Zoom, email 
councilremotetestimony@hawaiicounty.gov or call (808) 961-8255 to complete the registration process

September 30, 2024 
 
Submitting Written Testimony:  To ensure timely delivery to Council Members prior to the meeting,  
written testimony must be submitted no later than 12:00 noon on Monday, September 30, 2024, by:  
(1) email to counciltestimony@hawaiicounty.gov; (2) facsimile to (808) 961-8912; or (3) mail to the  
County Clerk’s Office in Hilo at 25 Aupuni Street, Hilo, Hawai‘i, 96720.  All written testimony,  
regardless of time of receipt, will be made part of the permanent record.  Please submit separate  
testimony for each item.

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 206: REPEALS ORDINANCE NO. 84-39 AND AMENDS CHAPTER 14, ARTICLE 8, 
SECTION 14-44, OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS 
AMENDED), RELATING TO NUCLEAR ENERGY    
Repeals Ordinance No. 84-39 enacted in 1984 to specify that United States military 
operations are exempt from and not to be impeded by prohibitions on the transportation 
or storage of radioactive material.
