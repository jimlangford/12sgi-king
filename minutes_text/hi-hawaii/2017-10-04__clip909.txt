Bill 52: ADOPTS THE COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I KA‘Ū COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT 
PLAN (CDP)  
The Windward Planning Commission forwards its favorable recommendation for the 
adoption of the Kaʻū CDP.  The CDP Program is established by the County’s General 
Plan to create a framework for residents and other stakeholders to participate in 
regional planning for their communities.  The planning area for the Kaʻū CDP 
encompasses most of Judicial District 9, surrounding Ocean View, Waiohinu, 
Nāʻālehu, and Pāhala. 
 
   Reference: Comm. 351 
Intr. by: Ms. David (B/R) 
   First Reading: September 20, 2017
