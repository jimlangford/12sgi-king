(Draft 2) 
APPROVES THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY COUNCIL LEGISLATIVE PROPOSALS 
AND PRIORITIES TO BE CONSIDERED FOR INCLUSION IN THE 
2025 HAWAIʻI STATE ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE 
PACKAGE  
Includes proposals relating to the regulation of tobacco products, State land use 
boundary amendments, denitrification in individual wastewater systems, regulating 
midwife licensures, advancing the timeline and providing more funding for 
cesspool conversions, extending the County surcharge of general excise tax, and 
regulating agricultural tourism, and 10 policy priorities relating to: (1) increasing 
funds for emergency preparedness; (2) lowering the cost and expanding the 
availability of insurance for homeowners and businesses; (3) workforce 
development; (4) expanding access to midwifery licensures; (5) game management 
of wild ungulates; (6) promoting producer responsibility for solid waste; 
(7) prioritizing and promoting safe routes to school; (8) increasing access to locally-
produced food; (9) improving public safety, expanding access to mental health 
resources, and establishing community courts; and (10) preventing squatters and 
supporting communities impacted by squatting.
