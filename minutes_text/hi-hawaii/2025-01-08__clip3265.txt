AUTHORIZES THE EMPLOYMENT OF A PRIVATE ATTORNEY TO

TARGET AND SIMILARLY SITUATED DEFENDANTS

Target and other potential defendants who may be liable to the County of Hawai‘i 
for their part in the opioid epidemic.
