ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 234-23: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF A LATER FISCAL YEAR AND OF 
MORE THAN ONE FISCAL YEAR FOR A MULTI-YEAR AGREEMENT FOR AN 
ENTERPRISE RESOURCE PLANNING SYSTEM   
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a 10-year agreement at an estimated annual cost of 
$2,200,000, for a software system used for accounting, procurement, project 
management, risk and compliance management, and supply chain operations. 
 
     Reference: Comm. 415 
     Intr. by: Mr. Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
Res. 235-23: CREATES ONE NEW BATTALION CHIEF AND THREE NEW FIRE HELICOPTER 
PILOT POSITIONS FOR THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY FIRE DEPARTMENT  
Establishes new pilot positions necessary to transition away from contracting services  
and adds a Battalion Chief position for the Emergency Operations Division. 
 
 Reference: Comm. 416 
 Intr. by: Mr. Kāneali‘i-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
Res. 236-23: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF A LATER FISCAL YEAR AND  
 OF MORE THAN ONE FISCAL YEAR FOR MULTI-YEAR EMPLOYMENT 
CONTRACTS FOR THE PLANNING DEPARTMENT   
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into four-year contracts for seven positions to deliver 
recovery efforts from the 2018 Kīlauea Eruption, to be funded with disaster recovery 
sources. 
 
     Reference: Comm. 418 
     Intr. by: Mr. Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 64:  AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 23-50, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING BUDGET 
FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE 30, 2024 
Increases revenues in Fund Balance From Previous Year account ($8,903,560.97); and 
appropriates the same to the Housing Production account to fund successful proposals  
for affordable housing production. 
 
  Reference:  Comm. 417 
 Intr. by: Mr. Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
