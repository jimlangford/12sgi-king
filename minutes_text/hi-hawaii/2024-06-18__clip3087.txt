testimony for each item.

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 165:  AMENDS CHAPTER 24, ARTICLE 10 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE  
  1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO PARKING   
  Changes the County Building parking lot located at 25 Aupuni Street in Hilo from  
  a two-hour metered parking zone to a two-hour parking area. 
      
     Reference: Comm. 884 
     Intr. by: Ms. Evans 
     Postponed: June 4, 2024 
 
   (Note:  There is a motion by Ms. Kimball, seconded by Ms. Kierkiewicz, to  
   recommend passage of Bill 165 on first reading.)
