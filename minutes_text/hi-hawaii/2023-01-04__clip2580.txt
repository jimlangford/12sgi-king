ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 22-23: AWARDS A PUBLIC ACCESS, OPEN SPACE, AND NATURAL RESOURCES 
PRESERVATION MAINTENANCE FUND STEWARDSHIP GRANT TO 
KOHANAIKI ‘OHANA  
Approves a grant of $20,380 to Kohanaiki ʻOhana, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, 
to protect, preserve, and restore the ‘O‘oma Beach property in North Kona, (Tax Map 
Key:  7-3-009-0004), pursuant to Section 10-16(h) of the Hawaiʻi County Charter. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 23 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba 
 
 
Res. 23-23: AWARDS A PUBLIC ACCESS, OPEN SPACE, AND NATURAL RESOURCES 
PRESERVATION MAINTENANCE FUND STEWARDSHIP GRANT TO 
PŌHĀHĀ I KA LANI  
Approves a grant of $39,710 to Pōhāhā I Ka Lani, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, 
to protect, preserve, and restore the Waipiʻo Valley Lookout property in Hāmākua, 
(Tax Map Key 4-8-004-006), pursuant to Section 10-16(h) of the Hawai‘i County 
Charter. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 24 
    Intr. by: Ms. Kimball 
 
 
Res. 24-23: AUTHORIZES THE DEPARTMENT OF RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT TO 
AWARD FUNDS TO THE FOOD BASKET  
Provides grant funds in the amount of $91,500, to cultivate fresh produce, reduce 
food waste, increase the volume of fruits and vegetables diverted to local food 
assistance programs, expand the consumption of local agricultural products, deepen 
engagement with Hawaiʻi Island’s food system, and heighten the program’s 
sustainability. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 25 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba (B/R)

Res. 25-23: AUTHORIZES THE DEPARTMENT OF RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT TO 
AWARD FUNDS TO HUI MĀLAMA I KE ALA ‘ŪLILI  
Provides grant funds of $100,000, to establish a community resilience hub in Pa‘auilo 
to address the current and future needs for food systems, a sustainable economy, and 
community care. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 26 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba (B/R) 
 
 
Res. 26-23: AUTHORIZES THE DEPARTMENT OF RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT TO 
AWARD FUNDS TO LA‘I‘ŌPUA 2020  
Provides grant funds of $100,000, to support the Ho‘okahua Food Preservation 
Project that will teach food preservation techniques, encourage the community to buy 
local produce, and support a food program for qualified students in West Hawaiʻi. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 27 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba (B/R) 
 
 
Res. 31-23: AUTHORIZES THE DEPARTMENT OF RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT TO 
AWARD FUNDS TO THE EDITH KANAKA‘OLE FOUNDATION  
Provides grant funds of $90,525, to support the KIPA (Known Indigenous 
Perspectives and Alignment) Business Cultural Training program. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 38 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba (B/R) 
 
 
Res. 32-23: AUTHORIZES THE DEPARTMENT OF RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT TO 
AWARD FUNDS TO THE EDITH KANAKA‘OLE FOUNDATION  
Provides grant funds of $100,000, to support the Kukulu ka Pahukapu Program and 
advance Responsible Tourism and Pono-Based Communication. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 39 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba (B/R) 
 
 
Res. 33-23: AUTHORIZES THE DEPARTMENT OF RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT TO 
AWARD FUNDS TO THE KOHALA CENTER  
Provides grant funds of $87,047, to support the Pūlama Kahalu‘u:  Supporting 
Ongoing Stewardship Program. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 40 
    Intr. by: Mr. Inaba (B/R)
