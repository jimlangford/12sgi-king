Bill 140:  AMENDS CHAPTER 20 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016  
EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO SOLID WASTE FACILITIES  
Requires that all solid waste facilities used for the disposal, collection, and 
transfer of household waste be operated and open to the public at least two days 
per week.

Bill 165:  AMENDS CHAPTER 24, ARTICLE 10 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE  
  1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO PARKING   
  Changes the County Building parking lot located at 25 Aupuni Street in Hilo from  
  a two-hour metered parking zone to a two-hour parking area. 
      
     Reference: Comm. 884 
     Intr. by: Ms. Evans 
 
 
 
Bill 167:  AMENDS CHAPTER 24, ARTICLE 10, DIVISION 5, SECTION 24-284,  
  SCHEDULE 32 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS  
  AMENDED), RELATING TO TWO-HOUR PARKING AREAS    
   Restricts parking in marked stalls along Highway 240 between Kika Street and  
   Ohelo Street in the Hāmākua District to two hours, between the hours of 8:00 a.m. 
   and 4:00 p.m., Monday through Saturday, excluding any observed holiday. 
       
      Reference: Comm. 892 
      Intr. by: Ms. Lee Loy (B/R)
