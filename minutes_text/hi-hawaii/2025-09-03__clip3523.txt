Bill 72: 
(Draft 2) 
INITIATES AN AMENDMENT TO ARTICLE VI, CHAPTER 10, 
SECTION 6-10.3, OF THE CHARTER OF THE COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I 
(2024 EDITION), RELATING TO THE DIRECTOR OF ENVIRONMENTAL 
MANAGEMENT  
Requires the Director of Environmental Management to have administrative 
experience in engineering, solid waste management, wastewater treatment, 
environmental policy and regulatory compliance, public administration, business, 
administration, or a related field; removes the requirement that the director needs to 
have an engineering degree or another degree in a related field. 
 
Reference: Comm. 400.2 
Intr. by: Council Members Kierkiewicz 
Approve: GOEAC-64

 Section 15-1(a), Hawai‘i County Charter

Bill 38: 
(Draft 2) 
AMENDS CHAPTER 19, ARTICLE 13, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 
1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO REAL PROPERTY 
TAX CREDITS  
Establishes a Private Road Tax Credit of up to $250 to be applied towards a 
homeowner’s real property tax bill when the homeowner expends at least $75 
annually to maintain, repair, or improve a private roadway that is not restricted by a 
gate and is managed by a road maintenance organization. 
 
Reference: Comm. 203.7 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder  
First Reading: August 20, 2025 
 
 
Bill 60: 
(Draft 4) 
AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLE 1, ARTICLE 2, ARTICLE 4, ARTICLE 5, 
AND ARTICLE 7, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, 
AS AMENDED), RELATING TO ZONING DISTRICT REGULATIONS FOR 
MEETING FACILITIES, CHURCHES, TEMPLES, SYNAGOGUES, AND 
COMMUNITY BUILDINGS (Planning Director Initiated)  
Removes existing zoning regulations for community buildings, churches, temples, 
and synagogues and creates new regulations for “meeting facilities” and “special 
events.”
