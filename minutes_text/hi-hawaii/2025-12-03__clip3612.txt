DEVELOPMENT TO AWARD FOUR GRANTS FOR PROGRAMS 
ADDRESSING AFFORDABLE HOUSING

Bill 107: 
 
AMENDS CHAPTER 17 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO THE FIRE CODE  
Adopts the State Fire Code as the County Fire Code; and adds administrative 
procedures relating to fire code enforcement, including permits, reviews, and 
inspections.

AMENDS CHAPTER 20 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO PLASTIC AND POLYSTYRENE 
WASTE  
Seeks to reduce disposable plastic or polystyrene foodware and serviceware in 
alignment with the County of Hawai‘i’s Zero Waste Plan and the State of 
Hawai‘i’s Aloha+ Challenge Solid Waste Reduction Goals. 
 
Reference: Comm. 482.10 
Intr. by: Council Members Galimba and Villegas 
First Reading: November 19, 2025 
 
 
 
Bill 89: 
(Draft 2) 
 
AMENDS CHAPTER 7, ARTICLE 1, OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 
1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT 
OF THE CIVIL DEFENSE ADMINISTRATOR  
Adds a new section requiring the Civil Defense Administrator have a minimum of 
three years of responsible administrative experience in emergency management, 
disaster planning, homeland security, or public safety services management.  In 
the event of a vacancy, the Mayor may convene a selection advisory panel 
consisting of the Fire Chief and Chief of Police to evaluate qualified applicants. 
 
Reference: Comm. 514.2 
Intr. by: Council Member Kierkiewicz 
First Reading: November 19, 2025
