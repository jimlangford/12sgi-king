ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 548-26: AWARDS FIVE STEWARDSHIP GRANTS FROM THE PUBLIC ACCESS, 
OPEN SPACE, AND NATURAL RESOURCES PRESERVATION 
MAINTENANCE FUND AS RECOMMENDED BY THE DIRECTOR OF 
FINANCE FOR THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE 30, 2027    
Authorizes the Director of Finance to provide Public Access, Open Space, and 
Natural Resources Preservation Maintenance Fund Stewardship grants totaling 
$477,266.50 to four nonprofit organizations, pursuant to Section 10-16(h) of the 
Hawaiʻi County Charter.  
 
Reference: Comm. 860 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

Res. 549-26: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS FROM LATER FISCAL YEARS 
TO ENTER INTO A MULTI-YEAR HOUSING ASSISTANCE PAYMENT 
CONTRACT WITH THE PROPERTY MANAGER OF AN AFFORDABLE 
HOUSING PROJECT SELECTED BY THE OFFICE OF HOUSING AND 
COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT FOR THE PROJECT-BASED VOUCHER 
PROGRAM           
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a multi-year agreement to provide 29 vouchers 
for the Kinoʻole Senior Residences proposed voucher project.  The estimated 
annual cost of the agreement would be up to $550,000, with a term of up to  
20 years.  
 
Reference: Comm. 861 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
 
Res. 550-26: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF LATER FISCAL YEARS  
OF COST ITEMS ASSOCIATED WITH THE HAWAIʻI FIRE FIGHTERS 
ASSOCIATION, BARGAINING UNIT 11, AND CERTAIN OTHER 
EMPLOYEES EXCLUDED FROM BARGAINING UNIT 11 FOR THE 
CONTRACT PERIOD BEGINNING JULY 1, 2025, AND ENDING  
JUNE 30, 2029         
The estimated costs over the four years of the new contract resulting from  
across-the-board increases would be $25,292,860. 
 
Reference: Comm. 862 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
 
Res. 551-26: AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN 
INTERGOVERNMENTAL AGREEMENT WITH THE STATE OF HAWAIʻI, 
DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE AND BIOSECURITY, TO RECEIVE A 
GRANT FOR THE COFFEE BERRY BORER AND COFFEE LEAF RUST 
PESTICIDE SUBSIDY PROGRAM         
Allows for the receipt of $150,000 of federally-derived funds to aid coffee  
growers in Kona and Kaʻū by offsetting the cost of purchasing approved  
pesticides.  
 
Reference: Comm. 863 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 153: 
 
AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-45, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET OF THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR  
ENDING JUNE 30, 2026         
Appropriates revenues in the State Grants – Coffee Berry Borer/Coffee Leaf Rust 
Pesticide Subsidy Program account ($150,000); and appropriates the same to the 
Coffee Berry Borer/Coffee Leaf Rust Pesticide Subsidy Program account.  Funds 
would used to aid coffee growers in Kona and Kaʻū by offsetting the cost of 
purchasing approved pesticides.  
 
Reference: Comm. 863 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
