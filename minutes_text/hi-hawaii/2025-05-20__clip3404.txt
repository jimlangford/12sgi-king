ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 180-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH  
THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAIʻI OFFICE OF RESEARCH SERVICES   
Allows the County to provide a $14,805 grant to the College of Tropical 
Agriculture and Human Resources to support its Master Food Volunteer Program. 
 
Reference: Comm. 289 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
Res. 181-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH  
THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAIʻI OFFICE OF RESEARCH SERVICES   
Allows the County to provide a $125,000 grant to the Pacific Aquaculture and 
Coastal Resource Center for permits and preliminary research to develop an 
aquaculture project in Hilo Bay. 
 
Reference: Comm. 290 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
Res. 182-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH  
THE STATE OF HAWAIʻI, HAWAIʻI HOUSING FINANCE AND 
DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION        
Allows for the receipt of $1,977,568 of federally-derived funds to be used towards 
the Kuakini Heights Affordable Housing Project.  
 
Reference: Comm. 292 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
Res. 183-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES INDEMNIFICATION OF CURRENT AND FORMER COUNTY 
EMPLOYEES NAMED AS INDIVIDUAL DEFENDANTS IN THE LAWSUIT 
KNOWN AS ARYN MITSUO YOSHIDA, ET AL. V. COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI 
ET AL., CIVIL NO. 3CCV-23-0000038 AGAINST ALL DAMAGES ASSESSED 
 
Reference: Comm. 293 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 54: 
 
AMENDS ORDINACE NO. 24-33, AS AMENDED, RELATING TO PUBLIC 
IMPROVEMENTS AND FINANCING THEREOF FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
JULY 1, 2024 TO JUNE 30, 2025        
Appropriates excess fuel tax revenues collected from fiscal years 2022-2023 and 
2023-2024 to the following projects totaling $1,493,554.75:  Roadway Projects 
($448,066.43), Bridge Inspection/Repair ($298,710.95), and Local Road Projects 
($746,777.37).  Funds for these projects shall be provided from fuel tax revenues, 
in excess of what was projected in Ordinance No. 23-18. 
 
Reference: Comm. 291 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
 
Bill 55: 
 
AMENDS ORDINACE NO. 24-32, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
ENDING JUNE 30, 2025         
Appropriates revenues in the Federal Grants – HOME-American Rescue Plan 
Program account ($1,977,568); and appropriates the same to the HOME-American 
Rescue Plan Projects account ($1,817,225) and HOME-American Rescue Plan 
Administration account ($160,343), to be used towards the Kuakini Heights 
Affordable Housing Project.   
 
Reference: Comm. 292 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
