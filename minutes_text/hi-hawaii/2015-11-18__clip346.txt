Bill 100 
(Draft 2): 
AMENDS CHAPTER 20, ARTICLE 5, SECTIONS 20-50 AND 20-52 OF  
THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983 (2005 EDITION, AS AMENDED), 
RELATING TO REFUSE  
Amendments would allow for the transport of refuse from transfer stations to the 
non-designated landfill if doing so would avoid penalty fees, help to meet 
minimum volumes to qualify for discounted fees, or if the designated landfill is 
under repair.

Intr. by: Ms. Wille
