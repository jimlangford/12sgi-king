ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 375-25: 
 
CREATES ONE HUMAN RESOURCES SPECIALIST II POSITION FOR THE 
DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN RESOURCES      
Establishes one new position to provide additional support to the department’s 
Classification and Pay Division.  
 
Reference: Comm. 585 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
 
Res. 376-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH  
THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE, NATIONAL 
OCEANIC AND ATMOSPHERIC ADMINISTRATION, TO RECEIVE A 
GRANT FOR THE PUAPUAʻA IKI HABITAT RESTORATION PROJECT  
FOR INVASIVE SPECIES MANAGEMENT AND SHORELINE 
STABILIZATION          
Allows for the receipt of $1,901,521 of federally-derived funds to restore eleven 
and a half acres of deforested land in Puapuaʻa Iki in North Kona.  
 
Reference: Comm. 586 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
 
Res. 377-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF LATER FISCAL YEARS  
FOR THE FUNDING OF COST ITEMS ASSOCIATED WITH THE STATE  
OF HAWAIʻI ORGANIZATION OF POLICE OFFICERS, BARGAINING  
UNIT 12, AND CERTAIN OTHER EMPLOYEES EXCLUDED FROM 
BARGAINING UNIT 12 FOR THE CONTRACT PERIOD BEGINNING  
JULY 1, 2025, AND ENDING JUNE 30, 2029      
The estimated costs over the four years of the new contract resulting from across-
the-board increases, step movements, and one-time lump sum bonuses would be 
$57,298,879.  
 
Reference: Comm. 589 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 97: 
 
AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-45, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET OF THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR  
ENDING JUNE 30, 2026         
Appropriates revenues in the Federal Grants – State Homeland Security Program 
Fiscal Year 2021 account ($2,189.48); and appropriates the same to the State 
Homeland Security Program Fiscal Year 2021 account, for a total appropriation of 
$622,189.48.  Funds will be used to support the waste-water bio-hazard mitigation 
project.  
 
Reference: Comm. 575 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
