conference technology (ICT) pursuant to Hawai‘i Revised Statutes §92-3.5.  Council Members will be

Members of the public may also attend from this location. 
 
Public attendance via Courtesy Site is available at the following locations: 
• Ocean View Community Association, 92-8924 Leilani Circle; 
• Pāhoa Council Office, 15-2879 Pāhoa Village Road; and

Oral Public Testimony via Zoom:  To provide oral testimony via Zoom, email 
councilremotetestimony@hawaiicounty.gov or call (808) 961-8255 to complete the registration process

March 17, 2026. 
 
Submitting Written Testimony:  To ensure timely delivery to Council Members prior to the meeting, written 
testimony must be submitted no later than 12:00 noon on Tuesday, March 17, 2026, by:  (1) email to 
counciltestimony@hawaiicounty.gov; (2) facsimile to (808) 961-8912; or (3) mail to the County Clerk’s 
Office in Hilo at 25 Aupuni Street, Hilo, Hawai‘i, 96720.  All written testimony, regardless of time of 
receipt, will be made part of the permanent record.  Please submit separate testimony for each item.

THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983 (2005 EDITION), BY CHANGING THE 
DISTRICT CLASSIFICATION FROM SINGLE FAMILY RESIDENTIAL –

10,000 SQUARE FEET (CN-10) AT WAIĀKEA HOUSELOTS, WAIĀKEA

(Applicant:  CU Hawaiʻi Federal Credit Union) (Area:  22,500 square feet)
