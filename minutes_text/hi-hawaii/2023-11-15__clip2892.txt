APPROVES LEGISLATIVE PROPOSALS AND PRIORITIES FOR INCLUSION 
IN THE 2024 HAWAIʻI STATE ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE 
PACKAGE

Regulation, and Emergency Management; and nine policy priorities relating to:  
(1) dangerous dogs; (2) the lawful sale or distribution of wild-caught ungulate meat; 
(3) pathways for midwifery licensure; (4) extended producer responsibility to 
address solid waste; (5) increasing access to mental health providers for youth in 
rural communities; (6) financing of residential cesspool conversions; (7) insurance 
coverage for properties located in Lava Zones 1 and 2; (8) insurance coverage for 
communities impacted by wildfires, hurricanes, and flooding; and (9) managing 
invasive species.
