BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 148:   DESIGNATES TAX MAP KEY:  6-6-001:010 AS “LAʻELAʻE PARK”, AMENDS 
CHAPTER 15, ARTICLE 8, SECTION 15-68.1, AND AMENDS CHAPTER 14, 
ARTICLE 1, SECTION 14-1 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO PARKS AND RECREATIONAL 
FACILITIES  
Adds Laʻelaʻe Park, located in Waimea, to the Parks and Recreational Facility Schedule 
and to Section 14-1 of the County Code, which prohibits the consumption of alcohol at 
any time.
