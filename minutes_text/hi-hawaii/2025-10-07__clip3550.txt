BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 86: AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-25, WHICH AMENDED SECTION 25-8-7 
(NORTH AND SOUTH KOHALA DISTRICT ZONE MAP), ARTICLE 8, 
CHAPTER 25 (ZONING) OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983  
 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), BY CHANGING THE DISTRICT 
CLASSIFICATION FROM AGRICULTURAL – 1 ACRE (A-1a) TO  
 SINGLE FAMILY RESIDENTIAL – 10,000 SQUARE FEET (RS-10)  
 AT ʻŌULI, SOUTH KOHALA, HAWAIʻI, COVERED BY TAX MAP  
 KEY:  6-2-001:075 (POR.) 
(Applicant:  Hawaiʻi Island Community Development Corporation) 
(Area:  14.321 Acres)          
The Leeward Planning Commission forwards its favorable recommendation for 
this request to amend Condition M (Affordable Housing) and Condition N (Fair 
Share Contribution), which would allow the applicant to facilitate their ability to 
utilize funds from the County’s Affordable Housing Production program to 
develop the ‘Koaiʻe’ affordable housing subdivision.  The property is located at 
the southwest corner of the intersection of Kawaihae Road and Waiula Drive. 
 
Reference: Comm. 507 
Intr. by: Council Member Kimball (B/R) 
 
; and 
Comm. 507.1: From Planning Director Jeffrey Darrow, dated September 10, 2025, transmitting a 
PowerPoint presentation for Bill 86.

AND 027 (PORTIONS) 
(Applicant:  Hawaiʻi Island Community Development Corporation)  
(Area:  18.38 Acres)          
The Leeward Planning Commission forwards its favorable recommendation for 
this request to amend Condition K (Affordable Housing) and Condition I (Fair 
Share Contribution), which would allow the applicant to utilize funds from the 
County’s Affordable Housing Production program to develop the final phases of 
the Kumakua Self Help Housing project.  The property is located along the makai 
side of the Akoni Pule Highway approximately 600 feet west of the Kohala High 
and Elementary School complex. 
 
Reference: Comm. 508
