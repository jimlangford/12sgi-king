FORMATION OF AN AD HOC COMMITTEE      
From Human Services and Social Services Committee (HSSSC) Chair Maile 
Medeiros David, dated February 1, 2017, describing the nonprofit grant review 
process and requesting formation of an ad hoc committee.  The ad hoc committee 
would conduct application reviews with the applicants for the County’s annual 
nonprofit grant awards and then report its findings to the HSSSC.
