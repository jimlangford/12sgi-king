AUTHORIZES THE EXEMPTION OF CERTAIN CODE REQUIREMENTS,

HAWAIʻI, COVERED BY TAX MAP KEY:  2-4-028:009 (Area:  3.2920 acres)

7,500 feet zoning requirements, to be replaced with Multiple-Family Residential
