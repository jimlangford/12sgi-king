ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 266-25: AUTHORIZES INDEMNIFICATION OF A FORMER EMPLOYEE OF THE 
COUNTY WHO IS NAMED AS AN INDIVIDUAL DEFENDANT IN THE 
LAWSUIT KNOWN AS DAVID J. FREEDMAN V. COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I,  
ET AL. AGAINST ALL DAMAGES ASSESSED THEREIN AND MAKING 
OTHER FINDINGS IN ACCORDANCE WITH SECTION 2-188 OF THE 
HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE         
Provides indemnification for Douglass S. Adams, former Director of the 
Department of Research and Development, regarding a civil action alleging claims 
against the County and Mr. Adams for employment discrimination, emotional 
distress, and negligent supervision. 
 
Reference: Comm. 428
