BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 50: AMENDS CHAPTER 14, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, 
AS AMENDED), BY ADDING A NEW ARTICLE RELATING TO CONCURRENT 
INSTALLATION OF ELECTRIC VEHICLE CHARGING STATIONS WITH THE 
INSTALLATION OF NEW GAS PUMPS AT RETAIL SERVICE STATIONS   
Requires the installation of an equal number of Alternating Current Level 2 Charging 
Stations as gas pumps anytime a new retail service station is established and the 
installation of an Alternating Current Level 2 Charging Station for each additional gas 
pump installed at an existing retail service station.  
 
 Reference: Comm. 306 
 Intr. by:  Mr. Inaba
