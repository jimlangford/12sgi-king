Res. 307-25: 
 
APPROVES THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY COUNCIL LEGISLATIVE PROPOSALS 
AND PRIORITIES TO BE CONSIDERED FOR INCLUSION IN THE 
2026 HAWAI‘I STATE ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE 
PACKAGE  
Includes proposals relating to repealing the preemption of local authority to regulate 
tobacco products; allowing the counties to request a State land use boundary 
amendment from agricultural to rural districts for small, contiguous parcels; 
requiring denitrification in individual wastewater systems under certain conditions; 
extending the county surcharge of the general excise tax until 2040; establishing a 
Homeless Services Fund to be funded through certain adjustments to the 
conveyance tax; and adding preferences for an applicant’s place of employment 
relative to the location of a housing project, status as a state or county employee, 
and status as a returning student when considering applications for housing 
assistance under Chapter 201H, Hawai‘i Revised Statutes.  Also includes eight 
policy priorities relating to:  (1) increasing funds for emergency preparedness; 
(2) increasing accessibility and transparency regarding the use of public funds for 
state transportation projects and highway maintenance; (3) workforce development; 
(4) water viability and usage, and watershed monitoring and stewardship; 
(5) responsible game management of wild ungulates; (6) promoting producer 
responsibility for solid waste; (7) wastewater infrastructure, cesspool conversion 
and related financing; and (8) prioritizing and increasing safe routes to school. 
 
Reference: Comm. 490 
Intr. by: Council Member Kimball 
Waived: GOEAC

Bill 38: 
(Draft 2) 
AMENDS CHAPTER 19, ARTICLE 13, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 
1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO REAL PROPERTY 
TAX CREDITS  
Establishes a Private Road Tax Credit of up to $250 to be applied towards a 
homeowner’s real property tax bill when the homeowner expends at least $75 
annually to maintain, repair, or improve a private roadway that is not restricted by a 
gate and is managed by a road maintenance organization. 
 
Reference: Comm. 203.7

Bill 72: 
(Draft 2) 
INITIATES AN AMENDMENT TO ARTICLE VI, CHAPTER 10, 
SECTION 6-10.3, OF THE CHARTER OF THE COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I
