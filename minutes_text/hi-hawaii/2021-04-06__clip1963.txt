ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 75-21:  URGES THE ADMINISTRATION TO CONDUCT A STUDY TO ADDRESS  
THE LACK OF AVAILABLE AND AFFORDABLE PROPERTY INSURANCE  
FOR RESIDENTS OF LAVA ZONES 1 AND 2  
Requests the Department of Finance to investigate and explore all possible  
mechanisms, including a captive insurance company, to provide affordable property 
insurance to “High Risk” properties within Lava Zones 1 and 2, and provide its  
findings by October 31, 2021.

 Intr. by:                 Mr. Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder
