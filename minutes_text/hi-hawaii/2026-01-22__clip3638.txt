LANDS FROM AGRICULTURAL – FIVE ACRES (A-5a) TO SINGLE-FAMILY

NORTH KONA, HAWAIʻI, COVERED BY TAX MAP KEY:  7-3-011:068 
(Applicant:  Ernest and Elizabeth Young) (Area:  1.86 acres)
