Res. 575-26:  
 
AUTHORIZES THE ACCEPTANCE OF ALL DONATIONS OF 
SECURITIES OR PERSONAL PROPERTY NOT COVERED UNDER 
THE CAPITALIZATION PROCEDURES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF 
FINANCE THAT ARE RECEIVED DURING THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING 
JUNE 30, 2027

Year 2026-2027.

AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN INTERGOVERNMENTAL 
AGREEMENT WITH THE STATE OF HAWAIʻI, DEPARTMENT OF

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES (FIRST READING) 
 
Bill 157: 
(Draft 2) 
AMENDS CHAPTER 7 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO DISASTER RECOVERY  
Establishes a County Disaster Recovery Program to coordinate short- and long-term 
recovery efforts following major disasters, led by a recovery officer appointed by 
the mayor with the responsibility for recovery planning, interagency coordination, 
community engagement, funding management, and the development of interim and 
long-term recovery and resilience plans. 
 
Reference: Comm. 888.4 
Intr. by: Council Member Kierkiewicz 
Approve: PCHSW-9 
 
 
 
 
 
Bill 162: AMENDS CHAPTER 3 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO PAID PARKING FACILITIES  
Adds a new article to establish signage requirements for paid parking facilities, to 
include a schedule of rates, notice of any additional fees, hours of operation, and 
information on towing procedures. 
 
Reference: Comm. 907 
Intr. by: Council Member Villegas 
Approve: PCPWMT-13

Bill 165: INITIATES AN AMENDMENT TO ARTICLE X OF THE CHARTER OF THE 
COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I (2024 EDITION), RELATING TO PUBLIC ACCESS, 
OPEN SPACE, AND NATURAL RESOURCES PRESERVATION  
Increases the annual allocation dedicated to the Public Access, Open Space, and 
Natural Resources Preservation maintenance fund from 0.25 percent to 0.5 percent 
of real property tax revenue; removes the accrual limit for the fund; provides that 
all expenses from the fund must align with the fund’s purpose; and authorizes the 
awarding of grants from the fund. 
 
Reference: Comm. 915 
Intr. by: Council Member Kierkiewicz 
Approve: GOEAC-139 
2/3 Vote: First of three required readings,  
 Section 15-1(a), Hawai‘i County Charter

Bill 152: 
 
INITIATES AN AMENDMENT TO ARTICLE III, SECTION 3-18, OF THE 
CHARTER OF THE COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I (2024 EDITION), RELATING TO 
THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY AUDITOR  
Clarifies that the County Auditor has the same power with respect to employees 
within the Office of the County Auditor as department heads in the Executive 
Branch and specifies that the County Auditor may appoint a deputy. 
 
Reference: Comm. 858 
Intr. by: Council Members Inaba and Kierkiewicz 
First Reading: May 20, 2026 
Second Reading: June 3, 2026 
2/3 Vote: Third of three required readings,  
 Section 15-1(a), Hawai‘i County Charter 
 
 
 
 
 
Bill 156: AMENDS CHAPTER 2, ARTICLE 25, SECTION 2-137, OF THE HAWAI‘I 
COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO 
APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS TO NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS
