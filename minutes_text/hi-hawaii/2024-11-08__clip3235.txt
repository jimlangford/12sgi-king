Bill 134: 
(Draft 3) 
 
AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLE 2, SECTION 25-2-42 OF THE HAWAIʻI 
COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO 
AMENDMENTS INITIATED BY PROPERTY OWNERS AND OTHER 
PERSONS  
Requires the Planning Director to submit a subject area report to the applicable 
planning commission and County Council along with each change of zoning district 
application.  The report would specify all changes of zone granted to surrounding 
properties, any incomplete and time sensitive conditions applied at the time of the 
change of zone, and the maximum number of lots into which each surrounding 
property could be subdivided.  Surrounding properties would include properties 
within 1,000 feet of the property for which the change of zone is sought for 
applications in the State Land Use Agricultural District, and properties within 
300 feet of the property for which the change of zone is sought for applications in 
the State Land Use Urban District or State Land Use Rural District. 
 
Reference: Comm. 741.10 
Intr. by: Mr. Inaba and Ms. Evans 
Referred to Planning 
Commissions: April 2, 2024 
First Reading: October 16, 2024
