ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 74-23: URGES AWARENESS AND PLEDGES SUPPORT TO PROTECT NATIVE  
 HAWAIIAN WOMEN AND GIRLS FROM ABUSE AND EXPLOITATION   
 Seeks to increase awareness of the Holoi Ā Nalo Wahine ʻŌiwi: Missing and  
 Murdered Native Hawaiian Women and Girls Task Force Report, and encourages the  
 community to promptly recognize indications of sex trafficking and abuse and to  
 immediately report any such instances to the proper authorities. 
 
  Reference: Comm. 151 
  Intr. by: Ms. Kagiwada and Ms. Lee Loy 
 
 ; and 
Comm. 151.1:      From Council Members Jennifer Kagiwada and Susan L. K. Lee Loy, dated  
 March 20, 2023, transmitting a request for a presentation by Nikki Cristobal, Ph.D.

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 32: AMENDS CHAPTER 2, ARTICLE 25, SECTION 2-138, OF THE HAWAIʻI  
                        COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO  
                         APPROPRIATION OF FUNDS TO NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS    
Clarifies that public funds may only be used for the actual costs of expenditures 
directly applicable to the service or activity covered by the grant award agreement and 
that public funds may not be used for the repayment of debts. 
 
                            Reference:   Comm. 149 
                                                                 Intr. by:         Ms. Kimball 
  Postponed: March 21, 2023 
 
(Note:  There is a motion by Ms. Kimball, seconded by Ms. Galimba, to 
recommend passage of Bill 32 on first reading.)
