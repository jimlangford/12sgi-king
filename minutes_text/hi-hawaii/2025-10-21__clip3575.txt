ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 343-25: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF LATER FISCAL YEARS TO 
ENTER INTO A LEASE AGREEMENT FOR ONE MULTIFUNCTION COPY 
MACHINE TO BE USED BY THE CIVIL DEFENSE AGENCY    
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a five-year lease agreement with an  
approximate cost of $3,000 per year.  
 
Reference: Comm. 543 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
 
 
Res. 344-25: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS FROM LATER FISCAL YEARS 
TO ENTER INTO A LICENSING AGREEMENT FOR PROPRIETARY 
COMPUTER SOFTWARE AND RELATED SERVICES TO BE USED BY  
THE DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY    
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a three-year agreement with an approximate  
annual cost of $934,000, for continued use of the Microsoft Enterprise Agreement 
program necessary for numerous County functions and operations. 
 
Reference: Comm. 544 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
 
 
Res. 368-25: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF LATER FISCAL YEARS TO 
ENTER A MULTI-YEAR AGREEMENT FOR PROFESSIONAL SERVICES 
RELATING TO THE IMPLEMENTATION OF A CAFETERIA PLAN UNDER 
SECTION 125 OF THE INTERNAL REVENUE CODE TO BE USED BY THE 
DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN RESOURCES      
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a one year and six month agreement, with 
options to extend for four subsequent one-year periods, with an approximate  
annual cost of $12,000, to furnish and implement a cafeteria plan consisting of 
health insurance premiums to be designated by the County as eligible for inclusion 
and two flexible spending accounts. 
 
Reference: Comm. 573 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 94: AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-45, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
ENDING JUNE 30, 2026         
Increases revenues in the Federal Grants – Emergency Solutions Grants Program 
account ($4,045); and appropriates the same to the Emergency Solutions Grants 
Projects account ($3,741) and Emergency Solutions Grants Administrative 
account ($304).   
 
Reference: Comm. 545 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
 
Bill 95: AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-45, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
ENDING JUNE 30, 2026         
Appropriates revenues in the Federal Grants – Block Grants account ($43,195)  
and decreases revenues in the Administrative Planning and Fair Housing account  
(-$19,305); and appropriates the same to the Community Development Block  
Grant Projects account, for a total appropriation of $62,500. 
 
Reference: Comm. 546 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
