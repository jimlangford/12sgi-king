Notice is hereby given that the Hawai‘i County Council’s Committee on Environmental Management 
held on November 1, 2016, was recessed and will reconvene as follows: 
 
 
DATE: November 15, 2016 
 
  
PLACE: West Hawaiʻi Civic Center 
74-5044 Ane Keohokālole Highway, Building A 
  Kailua-Kona, Hawaiʻi
