THE KĪLAUEA RECOVERY GRANT PROGRAM     
Provides $501,100 in grant funds to various nonprofit organizations to provide 
relief, recovery, mitigation, and long-term resilience for disaster damages, losses,

ADOPTS A PAY PLAN FOR PERSONS EMPLOYED BY THE COUNTY 
OTHER THAN THOSE WHOSE PAY IS OTHERWISE PROVIDED FOR, 
DESIGNATING PAYROLL PERIODS, PAY DAYS, PRESCRIBING 
REQUIREMENTS WITH RESPECT TO OFFICIAL FIDELITY BONDS,  
AND REPEALING ORDINANCE NO. 25-39 AND ALL AMENDMENTS 
THERETO, SAID ORDINANCE TO BE KNOWN AS THE SALARY 
ORDINANCE OF 2026

County agencies.
