ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 240-23:   APPROVES THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY COUNCIL LEGISLATIVE 
   PROPOSALS AND PRIORITIES TO BE CONSIDERED FOR INCLUSION IN  
   THE 2024 HAWAIʻI STATE ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE  
   PACKAGE                    
   Includes proposals relating to the regulation of tobacco and regulating traffic on  
   hazardous roads and in sensitive areas, and six policy priorities relating to: (1)  
   dangerous dogs; (2) the lawful sale or distribution of wild-caught ungulate meat;  
   (3) pathways for midwifery licensure; (4) extended producer responsibility to  
   address solid waste; (5) increasing access to mental health providers for youth in  
   rural counties; and (6) financing of residential cesspool conversions. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 426 
    Intr. by: Ms. Kimball
