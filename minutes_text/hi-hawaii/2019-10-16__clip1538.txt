(Draft 2) 
DECLARES A HAWAIʻI COUNTY CLIMATE EMERGENCY AND 
REQUESTS REGIONAL COLLABORATION TOWARDS AN IMMEDIATE 
JUST TRANSITION AND EMERGENCY MOBILIZATION EFFORT TO 
RESTORE A SAFE CLIMATE  
Seeks a commitment to rapidly complete the Hawaiʻi Island Climate Action plan, 
establish goals and objectives to reduce the use of fossil fuels, and support the

warming.

Bill 94: 
 
AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLES 2 AND 5, OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY 
CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO GENERAL 
ADMINISTRATION AND ZONING DISTRICT REGULATIONS FOR 
INDOOR AND MAJOR OUTDOOR AMUSEMENT AND RECREATION 
FACILITIES (Planning Director Initiated)  
The Windward and Leeward Planning Commissions forward their favorable 
recommendations for this amendment to the Zoning Code, which would remove 
the option to obtain a Use Permit to develop major outdoor amusement and 
recreational facilities in residentially-zoned districts, and add the development of 
indoor amusement and recreational facilities as a permitted use in the 
Neighborhood Commercial, Limited Industrial, and General Industrial zoning 
districts. 
 
Reference: Comm. 450 
Intr. by: Ms. Kierkiewicz (B/R) 
First Reading: October 2, 2019
