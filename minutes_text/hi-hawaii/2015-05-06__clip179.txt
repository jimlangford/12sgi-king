Res. 126-15 
(Draft 2): 
AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF A LATER FISCAL YEAR 
AND OF A MULTI-FISCAL YEAR AGREEMENT FOR FURNISHING 
ORGANIC WASTE DIVERSION OPERATIONS IN WEST HAWAI‘I AND 
EAST HAWAI‘I FOR THE DEPARMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL 
MANAGEMENT  
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into one or more ten-year contracts, with two 
five-year renewal options, to implement green waste collection sites in 
West Hawai‘i and/or East Hawai‘i. Any agreement would include an in-vessel 
composting component; may include an additional one year to the initial term for 
site preparation, mobilization and demobilization; and provide that all on-site 
improvements shall belong to the County upon the expiration of the contract 
period. 
 
Reference: Comm. 206.6 
   Intr. by: Ms. Eoff 
Held Over: April 15, 2015(Pursuant to  
 Council Rule 24(1)(e))
