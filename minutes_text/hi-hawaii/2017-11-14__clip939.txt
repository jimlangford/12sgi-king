ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 397-17: AUTHORIZES THE ACCEPTANCE OF A DONATION OF TWO “MOBILE 
MULTI” COUNTING DEVICES TO THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS  
Donated by the Hawai‘i Public Health Institute with a value of $8,000 each.  The 
department would initially install one device in East Hawai‘i and one device in 
West Hawai‘i to count pedestrians and bicyclists, and differentiate between the two, 
for statistical analyses of path and trail use patterns. 
 
   Reference: Comm. 588 
   Intr. by: Mr. Kanuha and Ms. Lee Loy

prior to the meeting.  The email shall indicate the appropriate Committee or Council meeting,

minutes in length.  Video testimony will not be played during the meeting; however, it will be

the right to refuse testimony containing inappropriate content or that is not in compliance with
