BILLS FOR ORDINANCES (FIRST READING) 
 
Bill 38: 
(Draft 2) 
AMENDS CHAPTER 19, ARTICLE 13, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 
1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO REAL PROPERTY 
TAX CREDITS  
Establishes a Private Road Tax Credit of up to $250 to be applied towards a 
homeowner’s real property tax bill when the homeowner expends at least $75 
annually to maintain, repair, or improve a private roadway that is not restricted by a 
gate and is managed by a road maintenance organization. 
 
Reference: Comm. 203.7 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder  
Approve: FC-77 
 
 
Bill 60: 
(Draft 3) 
AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLE 1, ARTICLE 2, ARTICLE 4, ARTICLE 5, 
AND ARTICLE 7, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, 
AS AMENDED), RELATING TO ZONING DISTRICT REGULATIONS FOR 
MEETING FACILITIES, CHURCHES, TEMPLES, SYNAGOGUES, AND 
COMMUNITY BUILDINGS (Planning Director Initiated)  
Removes existing zoning regulations for community buildings, churches, temples, 
and synagogues and creates new regulations for “meeting facilities and special 
events.” 
 
Reference: Comm. 313.30 
Intr. by: Council Member Kierkiewicz (B/R) 
Approve: PCPLUED-6

Bill 71: 
 
AMENDS CHAPTER 6 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 
(2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED) BY ADDING AN ARTICLE, RELATING 
TO NALOXONE  
Requires venues licensed to serve alcoholic beverages for in-person consumption to 
keep Naloxone on premises and ensure that employees are aware of its location and 
method of administration. 
 
Reference: Comm. 399 
Intr. by: Council Members Hustace and Inaba 
Approve: PCHSW-4

Bill 74: AUTHORIZES THE ISSUANCE OF A GENERAL OBLIGATION BOND OF 
THE COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I IN A PRINCIPAL AMOUNT NOT TO EXCEED 
TWO HUNDRED SEVENTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS FOR THE 
PURPOSE OF FINANCING A PORTION OF THE COSTS OF 
CONSTRUCTING A PUBLIC WATER SYSTEM SERVING IKI PLACE, 
KOHANAIKI HOMESTEADS, NORTH KONA, HAWAI‘I; FIXES THE FORM, 
DENOMINATION, AND CERTAIN DETAILS OF SUCH BOND; PROVIDES 
FOR THE ISSUANCE AND SALE OF SUCH BOND AND FOR OTHER
