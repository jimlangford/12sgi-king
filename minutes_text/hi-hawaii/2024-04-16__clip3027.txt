Oral Public Testimony via Zoom:  To provide oral testimony via Zoom, email 
councilremotetestimony@hawaiicounty.gov or call (808) 961-8255 to complete the registration process

April 15, 2024. 
 
Submitting Written Testimony:  To ensure timely delivery to Council Members prior to the meeting, 
written testimony must be submitted no later than 12:00 noon on Monday, April 15, 2024, by:  
(1) email to counciltestimony@hawaiicounty.gov; (2) facsimile to (808) 961-8912; or (3) mail to  
the County Clerk’s Office in Hilo at 25 Aupuni Street, Hilo, Hawai‘i, 96720.  All written testimony, 
regardless of time of receipt, will be made part of the permanent record.  Please submit separate 
testimony for each item.

ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 471-24: AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH 
THE HAWAI‘I EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT AGENCY (HI-EMA), 
PURSUANT TO HAWAI‘I REVISED STATUTES 46-7, FOR A GRANT TO 
THE HAWAI‘I FIRE DEPARTMENT  
Allows for the receipt of $33,200 to fund the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program-
Post Mana Road Fire program to reduce the risk of wildfires. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 797

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 146: AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 23-50, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR  
ENDING JUNE 30, 2024  
Appropriates revenues in the Federal Grants – Hazard Mitigation Grant Program – 
Mana Road Fire account ($33,200); and appropriates the same to the Hazard 
Mitigation Grant Program – Mana Road Fire account to reduce the risk of 
wildfires.
