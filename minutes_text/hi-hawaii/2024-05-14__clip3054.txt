ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 515-24: AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO A MULTI-YEAR  
AGREEMENT WITH THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAI‘I AT MĀNOA   
 Allows the Department of Research and Development to enter into a five-year 
agreement to share the cost of funding two Extension Agent positions who will 
cover Indigenous Crops/East Hawai‘i Master Gardeners and East Hawai‘i Livestock. 
 
   Reference: Comm. 872 
   Intr. by: Mr. Kāneali‘i-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
Res. 516-24: AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN AGREEMENT WITH THE 
STATE OF HAWAI‘I, DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE PURSUANT TO 
HAWAI‘I REVISED STATUTES 46-7, FOR A GRANT TO THE DEPARTMENT 
OF ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT  
Allows for the receipt of $200,000 of State funds to control green waste, palms, and 
other “host material” to minimize breeding grounds of the Coconut Rhinoceros Beetle.  
 
    Reference: Comm. 873 
 Intr. by: Mr. Kāneali‘i-Kleinfelder (B/R)

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 161: AMENDS CHAPTER 19, ARTICLE 11, SECTION 19-90 OF THE HAWAI‘I  
COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO  
REAL PROPERTY TAXES    
Expands permitted uses of Residential Tier Two Property tax revenue to include the 
funding of County-sponsored programs designed to address mental health. 
 
  Reference:  Comm. 858 
 Intr. by: Mr. Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder 
 
Bill 162: AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 23-50, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING  
BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING 
JUNE 30, 2024   
Appropriates revenues in the State Grants – Coconut Rhinoceros Beetle Grant account 
($200,000); and appropriates the same to the Coconut Rhinoceros Beetle Grant 
account, to control green waste, palms, and other “host material” to minimize breeding 
grounds of the Coconut Rhinoceros Beetle.  
 
  Reference:  Comm. 873 
 Intr. by: Mr. Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
