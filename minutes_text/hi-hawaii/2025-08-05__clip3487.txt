BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 71:   AMENDS CHAPTER 6 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, 
AS AMENDED) BY ADDING AN ARTICLE, RELATING TO NALOXONE  
Requires venues licensed to serve alcoholic beverages for in-person consumption to 
keep Naloxone on premises and ensure that employees are aware of its location and 
method of administration. 
 
 Reference: Comm. 399 
 Intr. by: Council Members Hustace and Inaba
