Res. 196-23: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF A LATER FISCAL YEAR AND OF 
MORE THAN ONE FISCAL YEAR FOR A MULTI-YEAR LICENSE AGREEMENT

AND RECREATION WITH EDMUND C. OLSON TRUST, OWNER OF TAX MAP 
KEY: 2-6-008:026 IN HILO, HAWAIʻI         
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a 10-year license agreement commencing on
