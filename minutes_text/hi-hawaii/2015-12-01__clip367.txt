BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 101: 
(Draft 2) 
INITIATES AN AMENDMENT TO ARTICLE XIV, SECTION 14-5 OF THE 
HAWAIʻI COUNTY CHARTER (2014), RELATING TO THE BOARD OF 
ETHICS  
The amendments would: 1) increase membership from five to nine members; 
2) decrease member’s term from five to four years; 3) add council district residency 
requirements; and 4) allow each council member to nominate two persons from his or 
her council district to serve on the board, from which one would be appointed by the 
mayor, subject to confirmation by the Council. 
 
   Reference: Comm. 494.4 
   Intr. by: Ms. Wille 
   Postponed: October 20 and November 17, 2015 
 
(Note:  There is a motion by Ms. Wille and seconded by Mr. Onishi 
to recommend passage of Bill 101, as amended, on first reading.)
