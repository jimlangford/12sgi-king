• Kona Council Chambers, West Hawai‘i Civic Center, 74-5044 Ane Keohokālole Highway, Building A 
Members of the public may also attend from this location. 
 
Public attendance via Courtesy Site is available at the following locations: 
• Ocean View Community Association, 92-8924 Leilani Circle; 
• Pāhoa Council Office, 15-2879 Pāhoa Village Road; and 
• Waimea Council Office, Parker Square, 65-1279 Kawaihae Road, Suite 109.

Oral Public Testimony via Zoom:  To provide oral testimony via Zoom, email 
councilremotetestimony@hawaiicounty.gov or call (808) 961-8255 to complete the registration process 
and obtain meeting login information.  Please contact our office to register by 12:00 noon, Tuesday,  
November 4, 2025. 
 
Submitting Written Testimony:  To ensure timely delivery to Council Members prior to the meeting, written 
testimony must be submitted no later than 12:00 noon on Tuesday, November 4, 2025, by:  (1) email to 
counciltestimony@hawaiicounty.gov; (2) facsimile to (808) 961-8912; or (3) mail to the County Clerk’s 
Office in Hilo at 25 Aupuni Street, Hilo, Hawai‘i, 96720.  All written testimony, regardless of time of 
receipt, will be made part of the permanent record.  Please submit separate testimony for each item.

Res. 345-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE MAYOR TO ENTER INTO AN INTERGOVERNMENTAL 
AGREEMENT WITH THE STATE OF HAWAIʻI, DEPARTMENT OF LAND 
AND NATURAL RESOURCES, DIVISION OF FORESTRY AND WILDLIFE, 
FOR MUTUAL AID AND PUBLIC SAFETY COORDINATION  
Authorizes the Hawaiʻi Fire Department to enter into a mutual aid agreement with 
the Department of Land and Natural Resources, Division of Forestry and Wildlife, 
to establish clear working protocols for resource sharing, unified command 
structures, and coordinated responses in the event of wildfires that exceed either 
department’s individual capacity. 
 
Reference: Comm. 547 
Intr. by: Council Member Kagiwada (B/R) 
Approve: GOEAC-76
