Public attendance via Courtesy Site is available at the following locations:

• Waimea Council Office, Parker Square, 65-1279 Kawaihae Road, Suite 109.

Oral Public Testimony via Zoom:  To provide oral testimony via Zoom, email 
councilremotetestimony@hawaiicounty.gov or call (808) 961-8255 to complete the registration process 
and obtain meeting login information.  Please contact our office to register by 12:00 noon, Tuesday,  
November 18, 2025. 
 
Submitting Written Testimony:  To ensure timely delivery to Council Members prior to the meeting, written 
testimony must be submitted no later than 12:00 noon on Tuesday, November 18, 2025, by:  (1) email to 
counciltestimony@hawaiicounty.gov; (2) facsimile to (808) 961-8912; or (3) mail to the County Clerk’s 
Office in Hilo at 25 Aupuni Street, Hilo, Hawai‘i, 96720.  All written testimony, regardless of time of 
receipt, will be made part of the permanent record.  Please submit separate testimony for each item.

Res. 395-25: 
 
APPROVES LEGISLATIVE PROPOSALS AND PRIORITIES FOR INCLUSION 
IN THE 2026 HAWAIʻI STATE ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE 
PACKAGE  
Includes proposals relating to emergency medical services and opioid use disorder 
treatment, mixed-use development relating to housing, cesspools, Department of 
Education recreational facilities, the Psychology Interjurisdictional Compact, 
tobacco products, the repeal of amendments to Section 201H-38 relating to housing, 
family, firearms, gun violence prevention, water pollution, general excise tax, 
conveyance tax, and housing for State and County employees; and nine policy 
priorities relating to:  (1) road usage charges for electric vehicles; (2) opioid use; 
(3) increasing funds for emergency preparedness; (4) workforce development; 
(5) water viability and usage, and watershed monitoring and stewardship; (6)  game 
management of wild ungulates; (7) promoting producer responsibility for solid 
waste; (8) wastewater infrastructure and cesspool conversion; and (9) prioritizing 
and promoting safe routes to school. 
 
Reference: Comm. 615 
Intr. by: Council Member Kimball 
Waived: GOEAC

(2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO THE APPOINTMENT OF
