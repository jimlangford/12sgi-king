ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 106-25: 
 
TRANSFERS/APPROPRIATES AN APPROPRIATION OUT AND FROM  
THE DESIGNATED FUND ACCOUNT(S) AND CREDITS SAME TO A 
DESIGNATED FUND ACCOUNT(S)       
Transfers $5,212 from the Parks and Recreation Administration Other Current 
Expenses account to the Animal Control account to provide a grant to Action 4 
Animals to support its rehoming and adoption programs for at-risk animals.  
 
Reference: Comm. 182 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 34: 
 
AMENDS CHAPTER 19, ARTICLES 1 AND 7, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY 
CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO THE 
AFFORDABLE RENTAL HOUSING REAL PROPERTY TAX 
CLASSIFICATION          
Repeals the definitions of “affordable rental housing” and “affordable rental rate,” 
and amends the requirements of eligibility for the affordable rental housing class. 
 
Reference: Comm. 163 
Intr. by: Council Member Inaba 
 
 
 
 
Bill 36: 
 
ADOPTS A PAY PLAN FOR PERSONS EMPLOYED BY THE COUNTY 
OTHER THAN THOSE WHOSE PAY IS OTHERWISE PROVIDED FOR, 
DESIGNATING PAYROLL PERIODS, PAY DAYS, PRESCRIBING 
REQUIREMENTS WITH RESPECT TO OFFICIAL FIDELITY BONDS,  
AND REPEALING ORDINANCE NO. 24-28 AND ALL AMENDMENTS 
THERETO, SAID ORDINANCE TO BE KNOWN AS THE SALARY 
ORDINANCE OF 2025         
Establishes rates of compensation for appointed employees in the Legislative 
Branch and the Office of Housing and Community Development, the County 
Physician, Student Helpers, and Senior Community Services Aids for various 
County agencies, and incorporates updates to minimum wage positions.  
 
Reference: Comm. 183 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

Bill 37: AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 24-32, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
ENDING JUNE 30, 2025         
Increases revenues in the Fund Balance from Previous Year account ($70,000,000); 
and appropriates the same to the Provision for Compensation Adjustment-General 
account.  Funds would be used to pay for Temporary Hazard Pay for all covered 
employees for work performed during the COVID-19 pandemic from March 2020 
to March 2022. 
 
Reference: Comm. 184 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
