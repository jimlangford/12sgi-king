ORDER OF RESOLUTIONS 
 
Res. 475-26: 
(Draft 2) 
AUTHORIZES AND DIRECTS THE DIRECTOR OF FINANCE TO 
NEGOTIATE THE DISPOSITION OF CERTAIN REAL PROPERTY BY 
LEASE TO NONPROFIT ORGANIZATIONS FOR PERMANENT HOUSING 
PURPOSES           
Authorizes the negotiation of six one-year leases with four one-year renewal 
options at an approximate annual cost of $10 for six residential properties to be 
used as long-term permanent housing for underserved populations.  
  
Reference: Comm. 744.4 
Intro. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
Postponed: March 3, 2026   
 
(Note:  There is a motion by Council Member Inaba, seconded by Council 
Member Kimball, to recommend adoption of Res. 475-26, as amended.) 
 
 
Res. 480-26: AUTHORIZES THE PAYMENT OF FUNDS OF LATER FISCAL YEARS TO 
ENTER INTO A MULTI-YEAR AGREEMENT WITH NEOGOV FOR 
SOFTWARE LICENSING, MAINTENANCE, AND SUPPORT FOR THE 
DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN RESOURCES      
Authorizes the Mayor to enter into a three-year agreement with an approximate 
annual cost of $34,100 for the first year, $36,570 for the second year, and $39,230 
for the third year, to provide software licensing, maintenance, and support for the 
department’s Recruitment and Examination Division. 
 
Reference: Comm. 756 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R) 
 
 
BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 139: 
 
AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-45, AS AMENDED, THE OPERATING 
BUDGET OF THE COUNTY OF HAWAIʻI FOR THE FISCAL YEAR  
ENDING JUNE 30, 2026         
Appropriates revenues in the Federal Grants – Benefits Enrollment Center – 
National Council on Aging account ($150,000); and appropriates the same to the 
Benefits Enrollment Center – National Council on Aging account.  Funds would  
be used to help enroll Medicare beneficiaries who have limited income and 
resources to access available benefits.  
 
Reference: Comm. 769 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
