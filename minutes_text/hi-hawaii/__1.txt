BILLS FOR ORDINANCES  
 
Bill 124: AMENDS SECTION 25-8-26 (PĀHOA ZONE MAP), ARTICLE 8, 
CHAPTER 25 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION,  
AS AMENDED) BY CHANGING THE DISTRICT CLASSIFICATION 
FROM VILLAGE COMMERCIAL – 10,000 SQUARE FEET (CV-10)  
AND  SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL – 10,000 SQUARE FEET (RS-10) 
TO DOWNTOWN PĀHOA COMMERCIAL (DPC) AT WAIAKAHIʻULA, 
PUNA, HAWAIʻI, COVERED BY THE AREA AS MORE PARTICULARLY 
DESCRIBED HEREIN  
Rezones certain areas in Pāhoa to a prospective special zoning district 
classification to be known as the Downtown Pāhoa Commercial District,  
which is proposed for establishment via Bill No. 126. 
 
Reference:   Comm. 708 
Intr. by:   Council Member Kierkiewicz 
Referred to Planning  
Commissions: February 4, 2026

Council Member Hustace, to recommend passage of Bill 124, on first  
reading.)
