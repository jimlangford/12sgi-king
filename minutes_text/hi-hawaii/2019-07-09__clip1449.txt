Res. 209-19: 
 
SUPPORTS THE EFFORTS OF GOVERNOR DAVID Y. IGE TO URGE THE 
BANK OF AMERICA TO RETURN TO HAWAI‘I TO MEET WITH NĀ POʻE 
KŌKUA AND THE HAWAI‘I FAIR LENDING COALITION REGARDING A 
$150,000,000 LOAN COMMITMENT FOR NATIVE HAWAIIANS ON 
HAWAIIAN HOMELANDS  
Requests that Bank of America address a 1994 order by the Federal Reserve 
System and Office of Thrift Supervision to make $150 million of loan funds 
available for mortgages on Hawaiian Homes Lands by 1998 and also address 
Hawai‘i Fair Lending Coalition’s claims of $360.6 million in opportunity costs and 
a $4.5 million late fee for failing to fulfill its required commitments. 
 
Reference: Comm. 344 
Intr. by: Ms. Kierkiewicz and Ms. Lee Loy 
Waived: FC
