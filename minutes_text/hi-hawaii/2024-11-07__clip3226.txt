Res. 693-24: APPROVES THE LEGISLATIVE PROPOSALS AND PRIORITIES FOR 
INCLUSION IN THE 2025 HAWAIʻI STATE ASSOCIATION OF  
 COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE PACKAGE  
Includes proposals relating to the regulation of tobacco products, deferred 
retirement for Police, the environment, cesspools, general excise tax,  
conveyance tax, and housing; and nine policy priorities relating to:  
(1) increasing funds for emergency preparedness; (2) lowering the cost and 
expanding the availability of insurance for homeowners and businesses;  
(3) workforce development; (4) game management of wild ungulates;  
(5) promoting producer responsibility for solid waste; (6) prioritizing and 
promoting safe routes to school; (7) increasing food security and access to  
locally-produced food; (8) improving public safety, expanding and access to 
mental health resources, and establishing community courts; and  
(9) preventing and combatting squatters and supporting property owners  
and communities impacted by squatters.
