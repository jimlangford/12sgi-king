Res. 630-24: APPROVES THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY COUNCIL LEGISLATIVE 
 PROPOSALS AND PRIORITIES TO BE CONSIDERED FOR INCLUSION IN  
 THE 2025 HAWAIʻI STATE ASSOCIATION OF COUNTIES LEGISLATIVE  
 PACKAGE          
Includes proposals relating to the regulation of tobacco products, State land use 
boundary amendments, denitrification in individual wastewater systems, and 
regulating midwife licensures, and 10 policy priorities relating to: (1) increasing 
funds for emergency preparedness; (2) extending the County surcharge of general 
excise tax; (3) lowering the cost and expanding the availability of insurance for 
homeowners and businesses; (4) advancing the timeline and providing more funding 
for cesspool conversions; (5) workforce development; (6) regulating agricultural 
tourism; (7) expanding access to midwifery licensures; (8) game management of 
wild ungulates; (9) promoting producer responsibility for solid waste; and  
(10) prioritizing and promoting  safe routes to school. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 1043 
    Intr. by: Ms. Kimball
