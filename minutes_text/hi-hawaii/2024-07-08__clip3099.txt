Bill 123: AMENDS CHAPTER 19, ARTICLE 1 AND ARTICLE 13, CHAPTER 23, 
ARTICLE 9, AND CHAPTER 25, ARTICLE 1, ARTICLE 2, ARTICLE 5, AND 
ARTICLE 6, OF THE HAWAIʻI COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, AS 
AMENDED), RELATING TO OHANA DWELLING UNITS

inserts the term “accessory dwelling unit”, and adds provisions for the
