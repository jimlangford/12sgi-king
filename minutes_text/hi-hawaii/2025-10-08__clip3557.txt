Res. 318-25: 
 
AUTHORIZES THE MASS TRANSIT AGENCY TO AWARD A GRANT  
TO PEOPLE FOR ACTIVE TRANSPORTATION HAWAIʻI    
Provides $240,000 of grant funds for the HIBIKE bike share program to cover  
day-to-day operations and maintenance. 
 
Reference: Comm. 510 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)

BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 91: 
 
AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-46, AS AMENDED, RELATING TO PUBLIC 
IMPROVEMENTS AND FINANCING THEREOF FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
JULY 1, 2025, TO JUNE 30, 2026        
Adds the Public Works Kahakai Boulevard Safe Routes to School - Keonepoko 
Project ($530,000) and the Kakakai Boulevard Safe Routes to School – 
Keonepoko – Federal Project ($2,100,000) to the Capital Budget.  Funds for these 
projects shall be provided from General Obligation Bonds, Capital Projects Fund – 
Fund  Balance and/or other Sources ($530,000) and Federal Grants Receivable 
($2,100,000).  
 
Reference: Comm. 539 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)  
 
 
 
Bill 92: 
 
AMENDS ORDINANCE NO. 25-46, AS AMENDED, RELATING TO PUBLIC 
IMPROVEMENTS AND FINANCING THEREOF FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
JULY 1, 2025, TO JUNE 30, 2026        
Increases the Public Works Mohouli Street Rehabilitation Project ($635,000) and 
the Mohouli Street Rehabilitation – Federal Project ($2,550,000) to the Capital 
Budget.  Funds for these projects shall be provided from General Obligation 
Bonds, Capital Projects Fund – Fund  Balance and/or other Sources ($635,000) 
and Federal Grants Receivable ($2,550,000).  
 
Reference: Comm. 540 
Intr. by: Council Member Kānealiʻi-Kleinfelder (B/R)
