BUDGET FOR THE COUNTY OF HAWAI‘I FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 
ENDING JUNE 30, 2019

Program account ($200,000); and appropriates the same to the Coffee Berry Borer 
Pest Subsidy Program account, bringing the total appropriation to $260,000.

used to control the Coffee Berry Borer.
