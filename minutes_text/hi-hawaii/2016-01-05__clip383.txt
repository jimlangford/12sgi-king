Bill 116: AMENDS CHAPTER 25, ARTICLE 1, ARTICLE 2, AND ARTICLE 4 OF THE 
HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2005 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO 
AGRICULTURAL TOURISM   
The bill would amend Sections 25-1-5(b), Definitions; 25-2-71(g), Applicability; and  
25-2-75, Plan approval application requirements; as they pertain to Agricultural 
Tourism.  Also would amend Section 25-4-15, Agricultural Tourism, to add and amend 
definitions and types of use for “major” and “minor” agricultural tourism activities; and 
sets forth specific regulations for each. 
 
    Reference: Comm. 554 
    Intr. by: Ms. Wille 
    Postponed: November 17 and December 1, 2015

Bill 124: AMENDS CHAPTER 16, ARTICLE 3, SECTION 16-5 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY 
CODE 1983 (2005 EDITION, AS AMENDED), RELATING TO COMMUNITY 
DEVELOPMENT PLAN ACTION COMMITTEES (CDPAC)  
The amendments would: 1) Allow a Council Member to appoint members to fill vacant 
positions if the CDPAC serves within that Council Member’s Council District and the 
position has been vacant for at least three months, subject to confirmation by the 
Council; 2) Allow a CDPAC member to serve up to three terms; and 3) Require that 
vacancies be filled before replacing a CDPAC member whose term has expired, if that 
member is willing to continue to serve until the vacancies are filled.  
 
    Reference: Comm. 601 
    Intr. by: Ms. Wille
