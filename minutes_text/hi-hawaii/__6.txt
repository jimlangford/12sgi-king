BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 162: AMENDS CHAPTER 3 OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 EDITION, 
AS AMENDED), RELATING TO PAID PARKING FACILITIES  
Adds a new article to establish signage requirements for paid parking facilities, to 
include a schedule of rates, notice of any additional fees, hours of operation, and 
information on towing procedures. 
 
Reference: Comm. 907
