BILLS FOR ORDINANCES 
 
Bill 67: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
AMENDS SECTION 25-8-33 (CITY OF HILO ZONE MAP)  ARTICLE 8, 
CHAPTER 25 (ZONING) OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), BY CHANGING THE ZONE MAP 
CLASSIFICATION FROM SINGLE FAMILY RESIDENTIAL -10,000  
SQUARE FEET (RS-10) TO GENERAL COMMERCIAL-10,000 SQUARE  
FEET (CG-10) AT WAIĀKEA, SOUTH HILO, HAWAIʻI, COVERED BY  
TAX MAP KEY:  2-2-022:022 
(Applicant:  Correa Ohana, LLC) (Area:  33,180 square feet)  
The Windward Planning Commission forwards its favorable recommendation for 
the requested change of zone, which would allow the applicant to demolish the 
existing single-family dwelling and garage and construct a multi-family apartment 
complex.  The property is located at 1198 Kinoʻole Street.  
  
Reference: Comm. 375 
Intr. by: Council Member Kimball  (B/R)  
Postponed: July 22, 2025 
 
(Note:  There is a motion by Council Member Kagiwada, seconded by 
Council Member Hustace, to recommend passage of Bill 67 on first reading.)

Bill 73: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Comm. 407.1: 
AMENDS SECTION 25-8-33 (CITY OF HILO ZONE MAP), ARTICLE 8, 
CHAPTER 25 (ZONING) OF THE HAWAI‘I COUNTY CODE 1983 (2016 
EDITION, AS AMENDED), BY CHANGING THE ZONE MAP 
CLASSIFICATION FROM SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL - 15,000 
SQUARE FEET (RS-15) TO SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL - 10,000 
SQUARE FEET (RS-10) AT WAIĀKEA, SOUTH HILO, HAWAIʻI, COVERED 
BY TAX MAP KEY:  2-4-011:153 
(Applicant:  Douglas and Shelley Uyeda) (Area:  21,778 square feet)  
The Windward Planning Commission forwards its favorable recommendation for 
the requested change of zone, which would allow the applicant to subdivide the 
property into two lots and separate the two existing single-family dwellings 
currently on the property, enabling each dwelling to exist on its own lot.  The 
property is located at 461 Kehaulani Street.  
  
Reference: Comm. 407 
Intr. by: Council Member Kimball  (B/R)
