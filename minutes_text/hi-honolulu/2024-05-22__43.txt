 PROPOSED CD1 TO RESOLUTION 24-81 (Submitted by Councilmember Tupola) - The CD1 (OCS2024-0529/5/15/2024 4:44 PM) makes the following amendments: 

 FOR ADOPTION 

 A. Amends the sixth WHEREAS clause to clarify the restrictions imposed on landfill siting.

 B. Adds a newly ordered seventh WHEREAS clause to clarify the Landfill Advisory Committee’s role in the landfill site selection process.

 C. Adds a newly ordered eighth WHEREAS clause to insert language noting the City’s failure to provide an alternative landfill site.

 D. Adds a newly ordered thirteenth WHEREAS clause that emphasizes the accumulation of trash and debris from unpermitted camping at Leeward O?ahu parks.

 E. Removes language in the BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED clause and replaces it with language that requests the Department of Environmental Services to: (1) commit to four annual cleanups designated by the Council District 1 community; (2) review and evaluate options for leasing property to create a new convenience center to allow for additional bin space in Wai?anae; and (3) provide a communication to the Council regarding alternative sites being considered for a new landfill and any impacts to the Board of Water Supply No Pass Zone or to Act 73, Session Laws of Hawaii 2020.

 F. Adds another BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED clause that requests the City Administration to reconvene the Landfill Advisory Committee.

 G. Makes various technical and nonsubstantive amendments.
