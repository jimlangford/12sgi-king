 PUBLIC PARTICIPATION AND TESTIMONY

 VIEWING THE MEETING AND RESTRICTIONS ON ENTRY 

 Members of the public will not be allowed into the meeting room, but may view the meeting on a live broadcast. The meeting will be viewable: (1) by internet live streaming through olelo.org and (2) by televised live broadcast on Olelo TV Channel 54. 

 After the meeting, the meeting will be viewable on demand at https://www.honolulucitycouncil.tv/ . Copies of older meeting videos may be requested by calling the City Clerk’s Office at (808) 768‑5822, charges may apply.

 FOR ACTION 

 1.  BILL 48 (2021) – UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANKS. Relating to underground storage tanks. (Bill passed first reading on 12/15/21) 

 PROPOSED CD1 TO BILL 48 (2021) (Submitted by Councilmember Cordero) – The CD1 (OCS2022-0007/1/5/2022 8:01 AM) makes the following amendments: 

 A. In SECTION 2 of the bill, the CD1: 

 1. Adds a new Section 41-45.1 ("Definitions") amplifying former Section 4145.1(a) and adding specific definitions for "Department" and "Department of Health." 

 2. Provides that the new Article 45 in ROH Chapter 41 relating to underground storage tanks ("USTs") will be implemented and enforced by the department or agency designated by the Mayor. 

 3. Provides that the standard for granting or renewal of a UST permit will be whether the UST or UST system will be protective of human health and the environment, rather than a determination that the UST or UST system will not leak any regulated substance into the environment during its operating life. 

 4. Adds new ROH Sections 41-45.3 ("Underground storage tank permit procedures"), 41-45.4 ("Inspections"), 41-45.5 ("Notification and reporting"), and 41-45.6 ("Administration and enforcement"). 

 5. The rulemaking provision has been moved from Section 41-45.1(d) to Section 41-45.6(d).
