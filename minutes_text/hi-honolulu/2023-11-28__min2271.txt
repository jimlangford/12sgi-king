 2:30 P.M. 

 PUBLIC PARTICIPATION AND TESTIMONY 

 Pursuant to Section 92-3.7, Hawaiʻi Revised Statutes, this meeting will be conducted as a remote meeting by interactive conference technology, with the following procedures in effect for the meeting: 

 VIEWING THE MEETING 

 The meeting will be viewable: (1) by internet live streaming through https://www.honolulucitycouncil.org/meetings ; (2) by televised live broadcast on ‘Ōlelo TV Channel 54; and (3) on the monitor situated outside the Council Chamber. Viewers who experience a loss of viewing signal should try switching to another viewing option. 

 After the meeting, the meeting will be viewable on demand at https://www.honolulucitycouncil.org/meetings . Copies of older meeting videos may be requested by calling the City Clerk’s Office at (808) 768‑5822, charges may apply. 

 Some Councilmembers and presenters may be participating in the meeting by interactive conference technology from remote locations. 

 ORAL TESTIMONY 

 Oral testimony will be permitted on all items on the agenda. Each speaker may not have anyone else read their statement and is limited to a one‑minute presentation. 

 Remote Testimony 

 1.  For direct access to submit oral testimony call: +1-253-215-8782, enter ID:  86724980518 and Passcode: 112527 . 

 2.  To testify by videoconference visit: https://hnldoc.ehawaii.gov/hnldoc/testimony . Videoconference access information will be provided upon registration. Testifiers are encouraged to register/submit testimony at least 24 hours prior to the meeting.

 In-Person Testimony in the Council Chamber 

 Persons wishing to testify are requested to register by 2:30 p.m. by filling out the registration form in person outside the Council Chamber. Persons who have not registered will be given an opportunity to speak following the oral testimonies of the registered speakers. 

 Oral testimony will be allowed when each item is taken up in the following order: 

 1.  In-person testimony in the Council Chamber; 

 2.  Remote testimony. 

 WRITTEN TESTIMONY 

 Written testimony may be uploaded at https://hnldoc.ehawaii.gov/hnldoc/testimony , or mailed to Office of the City Clerk, Attention: Information Section, 530 South King Street, Room 100, Honolulu, HI 96813. If submitted, written testimonies, including the testifier’s address, e-mail address, and phone number, will be available to the public at https://hnldoc.ehawaii.gov . 

 Should you have any questions, please call (808) 768-3119 or send an email to irene.limos@honolulu.gov .

 PROPOSED CD1 TO BILL 56 (2023) ( Submitted by Council Chair Waters) – The CD1 (OCS2023-0994/10/31/2023 2:18 PM) makes the following amendments: 

 A. In SECTION 1 of the bill, replaces the term "loans" with "financing," specifies that the C-PACE program is a potential tool to address critical issues affecting the City, and states that fire sprinkler systems are a critical issue affecting the City. 

 B. In SECTION 2 of the bill: 

 1. Amends proposed ROH § 2-__.1 ("Definitions") as follows: 

 a. Amends the first sentence by replacing the word "chapter" with "article." 

 b. Amends the definition of "C-PACE Assessment" by removing the reference to the assessment being levied on a benefitted property at the request of the owner of a qualifying property, replacing the word "chapter" with "article," and removing the references to HRS Part IV of Chapter 196 and to HRS § 46‑80(b). 

 c. Changes the defined term "C-PACE Loan" to "C-PACE Financing" and amends the definition of the term by replacing the word "loan" with "financing," replacing the term "project" with "qualifying improvement," replacing the word "chapter" with "article," and removing the references to Part IV of HRS Chapter 196 and to HRS § 46-80(b). Re-alphabetizes this term in the list of defined terms. 

 d. Changes the defined term "C-PACE Loan Agreement" to "C‑PACE Financing Agreement." Re-alphabetizes this term in the list of defined terms. 

 Page 4 

 e. Amends the definition of "C-PACE Lender" by replacing the term "loan" with "financing." 

 f. Amends the definition of "C-PACE Program" to specify that the C-PACE assessment is levied by the City and collected by the Hawaii Green Infrastructure Authority (instead of by the City), by replacing the word "chapter" with "article," and by removing the references to Part IV of HRS Chapter 196 and to HRS § 46-80(b). 

 g. Amends the definition of "Qualifying Improvement" by replacing the term "authority" with "program administrator." 

 2.  Amends proposed ROH § 2-_.2 ("C-PACE program") by replacing the word "chapter" with "article." 

 3.  Amends proposed ROH § 2-_.3(a) by replacing the word "chapter" with "article" and removing the references to Part IV of HRS Chapter 196 and to HRS § 46-80(b). 

 4.  Amends proposed ROH § 2-_.3(b) by replacing the word "chapter" with "article," removing the references to Part IV of HRS Chapter 196 and to HRS § 46-80(b), and replacing the term "loan" with "financing." 

 5.  Amends proposed ROH § 2-_.3(c) by replacing the word "chapter" with "article," removing the references to Part IV of HRS Chapter 196 and to HRS § 46-80(b), replacing the term "loan" with "financing," and specifying that the responsibilities of the program administrator may include the billing and collection of C-PACE assessments. 

 6.  Amends proposed ROH § 2-_.4(b) to specify that the Council approval of the C-PACE administration agreement shall be by resolution. 

 C. Makes miscellaneous technical and nonsubstantive amendments.
