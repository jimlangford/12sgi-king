OF THE LATE ERNEST "ERNIE" REZENTS. WHEREAS, ERNEST "ERNIE" REZENTS, ONE OF THE MOST 
RESPECTED ARBORISTS IN THE HISTORY OF HAWAI'I, PASSED AWAY ON JUNE 13, 2023 AT THE AGE 
OF 91 IN WAILUKU SURROUNDED BY HIS FAMILY; AND WHEREAS, ERNIE WAS BORN ON JAN 17, 1932 
AT MAUI PU'UNENE HOSPITAL TO ERNEST AND VICTORIA REZENTS AND WAS RAISED IN PUHALA 
VILLAGE IN WAI'EHU; AND WHEREAS, ERNIE GRADUATED FROM ST.\r\n\r\nANTHONY SCHOOL IN 
WAILUKU IN 1950 AND BEGAN ATTENDING UNIVERSITY OF HAWAI'I AT MANA'O TO STUDY 
SECONDARY EDUCATION; AND WHEREAS, ERNIE'S FIRST TEACHING JOB WAS IN HONOKA'A, 
HAWAI'I ISLAND WHERE HE MET ALENE HUMPHRIES THE LOVE OF HIS LIFE AND ERNIE AND ALENE 
MARRIED INN 1958 AND PURCHASED A HOME IN MAKAWAO, WHERE THE COUPLE RAISED FIVE 
CHILDREN, CULTIVATED AN URBAN FOREST AND ESTABLISHED A GATHERING PLACE FOR THEIR 10 
GRANDCHILDREN AND THREE GREAT-GRANDCHILDREN; AND WHEREAS, IN 1960, ERNIE BEGAN 
TEACHING AT MAUI TECHNICAL SCHOOL, NOW KNOWN AS UNIVERSITY OF HAWAI'I - MAUI 
COLLEGE STARTING A CAREER THAT SPANNED FOR MORE THAN 50 YEARS IN WHICH HE TAUGHT 
COURSES SUCH AS "ALL ABOUT TREES"; AND WHEREAS, ERNIE RETURNED TO SCHOOL TO MAJOR 
IN BIOLOGY AT UNIVERSITY OF OREGON, AND TOOK COURSES IN HORTICULTURE AT THE 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, DAVIS DEVELOPING A RESPECT FOR AND EXPERTISE IN TREES; AND 
WHEREAS, AFTER RETURNING HOME, ERNIE WAS INSTRUMENTAL IN ESTABLISHING THE 
UNIVERSITY OF HAWAI'I MAUI COLLEGE'S AGRICULTURAL CAREERS PROGRAM AND 
SUCCESSFULLY LOBBIED THE STATE FOR FUNDING TO CONSTRUCT A CLASSROOM AND 
GREENHOUSE FACILITY TO TEACH HORTICULTURE, ARBORICULTURE AND OTHER PLANT-
MANAGEMENT COURSES; AND WHEREAS, ERNIE COMPLAINED HIS LOVE TREES TO MAUI GREEN

STREET AND PARK TREES AND TO IDENTIFY EXCEPTIONAL TREES; AND WHEREAS, IN 2016 ERNIE 
RESEARCHED, WROTE AND COORDINATED THE CURRENT THIRD EDITION OF THE MAUI COUNTY 
PLANTING PLAN, A 243-PAGE GUIDE FOR CARING, PRUNING, PLANTING REPLANTING, REMOVING
