CR 26-24 Recommending REFERRAL of bills to the Budget, Finance, and 
Economic Development Committee including: 
 
1. Bill 110 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE AMENDING 
SECTION 3.48.361, MAUI COUNTY CODE, RELATING TO REAL 
PROPERTY DEDICATED AS ʻĀINA KŪPUNA," to limit eligibility for 
ʻĀina Kūpuna real property tax relief to properties that qualify by 
January 1, 2026; 
 
2. Bill 112 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE AMENDING 
CHAPTER 3.48, MAUI COUNTY CODE, RELATING TO GOLF 
COURSE ASSESSMENT," to revise real property tax assessments for 
golf courses; 
 
3. Bill 114 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND 
CHAPTER 3.48, MAUI COUNTY CODE, RELATING TO THE REAL 
PROPERTY TAX ASSESSMENT OF NONTAXABLE PROPERTY," to 
clarify that vacant Section 207 leasehold land leased under Section 
207 of the Hawaiian Homes Commission Act, 1920, as amended, is 
exempt from real property tax, inc luding the minimum tax, and to 
limit tax on Section 207 improvements to the minimum tax; 
 
 4 
4. Bill 143 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE AMENDING 
SECTION 3.48.305, MAUI COUNTY CODE, TO ESTABLISH THE 
VACANT RESIDENTIAL REAL PROPERTY TAX CLASSIFICATION," to 
establish a vacant residential real property tax classification for 
unimproved residential and rural parcels without agricultural 
activity; 
 
5. Bill 144 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE AMENDING 
CHAPTER 3.48, MAUI COUNTY CODE, EXTENDING MAUI 
WILDFIRES REAL PROPERTY TAX RELIEF," to extend Maui wildfire 
real property tax relief for eligible Lahaina properties; 
 
6. Bill 145 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE ADDING A 
NEW SECTION TO CHAPTER 3.48, MAUI COUNTY CODE, 
ESTABLISHING AN EXEMPTION FOR VACANT LANDOWNERS ON 
THE UPCOUNTRY WATER SERVICE PRIORITY LIST," to establish a 
real property tax exemption for up to $50,000 for vacant Upcountry 
parcels on the water service priority list; 
 
7. Bill 146 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE AMENDING 
SECTION 3.48.410, MAUI COUNTY CODE, ON THE PRINCIPAL 
HOME EXEMPTION’S APPLICATION DEADLINES," to allow the 
home exemption to apply to the next tax payment if the claim is filed 
by December 31 or June 30; and 
 
8. Bill 181 (2025), entitled "A BILL FOR AN ORDINANCE AMENDING 
CHAPTER 3.48, MAUI COUNTY CODE, INCREASING THE AMOUNT 
OF THE HOME AND LONG -TERM RENTAL EXEMPTIONS FROM 
REAL PROPERTY TAX," to increase the home and long -term rental 
exemption amounts from real property tax.
