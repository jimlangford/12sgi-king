beginning January 2, 2025, relating to: (1) amending Subsection 2.96.060(B), 
Maui County Code, to amend the residential workforce housing deed restrictions 
to increase the time period for all restrictions; (2) amending Chapter 3.35, Maui 
County Code, to provide a stable and continuing source of funding to be used 
for planning, administering, and constructing affordable and workforce housing 
projects and to provide a policy for the use of the funding; (3) establishing an 
ordinance that restarts the deed -restricted period when a residential workforce 
housing unit is sold during the deed-restricted period; (4) amending the eligibility 
criteria for rental residential workforce housing units to exp and eligibility to 
individuals or families displaced by an emergency under Chapter 127A, Hawai‘i 
Revised Statutes or who are income and asset qualified but held a disqualifying 
interest in real property within the last three years; (5) a Change the Zoning, with 
conditions, of properties related to Lanai Project District 2 (Koele), at Lānaʻi City, 
Lānaʻi, Hawaii, and an amendment to Chapter 19.71, Maui County Code, 
relating to Lanai Project District 2 (Koele); (6) increasing the deed -restriction 
period for residential workforce housing ownership units and expediting the 
County's purchase of residential w orkforce housing units when offered to the 
County; (7) increasing the allowable density in the R-1, R-2, and R-3 Residential 
Zoning Districts to provide additional housing opportunities in Maui County; (8) 
providing lower -cost opportunities for more autonomous living areas in a 
dwelling unit by allowing for kitchenettes in dwelling units in the Residential and 
Rural Districts on Maui and Lana ʻi and requiring a dwelling unit with a 
kitchenette to be occupied on a long -term residential basis; and (9) addressing 
nonconforming structures and uses damaged, destroyed, or affected by an 
emergency or disaster declared by proclamation of the Governor to  constitute a 
state of emergency under Chapter 127A, Hawai‘i Revised Statutes.
