CR 24 -103 Recommending FIRST READING of Bill 141, CD1 (2024), to: 1) 
increase the rates of fare taxicab drivers, owners, licensees, and operators may 
charge; 2) authorize a $10 additional charge for the transport of each motorized 
wheelchair unaccompanied by its us er, large pet crate, bicycle, and surfboard; 
3) require no charge for the transport of motorized and non -motorized 
wheelchairs when accompanied by their users; 4) authorize a $5 airport pick-up 
fee; 5) establish a $20 flat rate when the distance traveled is three miles or less; 
6) provide a 20 percent fare discount for passengers not travelling together but 
agreeing to share the taxicab to the same destination or along the same route; 
and 7) delete provisions relating to signage for a taxicab in personal use and not 
for hire.
