MORNING, COUNCIL MEMBERS.\r\n\r\nTHE TITLE OF MY REMARKS THIS MORNING IS "SOUTH
MAUI, NAVIGATING DEVELOPMENT, CHALLENGES, AND OPPORTUNITIES." IF YOU HAVE DRIVEN
THROUGH KIHEI IN FEBRUARY, AS I'M SURE MANY OF YOU HAVE, YOU HAVE LIKELY EXPERIENCED
WHAT HAS BECOME A PAINFUL REALITY, SEVERE TRAFFIC CONGESTION. THIS TRAFFIC CONDITION
GESTION RECENTLY AMPLIFIED BY THE SOUTH KIHEI ROAD CLOSURE AND OVERRELIANCE PI'ILANI
HIGHWAY. MAKES ONE THING UNDENIABLE CLEAR SOUTH MAUI REQUIRE A MORE ROBUST AND
DIVERSIFIED ROAD NETWORK NOT JUST FOR TODAY'S CHALLENGES ABOUT FIFTH CIRCUIT COURT
SUSTAINABLE GROWTH IN THE YEARS TO COME. WE CANNOT CONTINUE TO DEPEND ON A SINGLE
ARTERY FOR ACCESS. DURING THE SOUTH KIHEI ROAD SHOUT DOWN COMMUTE TIMES ON PI'ILANI
HIGHWAY HAVE DOUBLED EVEN TRIPLED FROM WHAT WAS GENERAL 5-10 MINUTE DRIVE HAS BECOME
30-40 MINUTES. THIS TRAFFIC IS A ROLLOUT OF SOUTH KIHEI ROAD BEING CLOSED DUE TO THE
DAMAGE FROM THE RECENT FLOODING. THE IMPACT OF ONLY HAVING THE PI'ILANI HIGHWAY FOR
ACCESS INTO AND OUT OF SOUTH MAUI HAS UNDERSTANDABLY PROMPTED SERIOUS QUESTIONS
ESPECIALLY AS WE CONSIDER SEVERAL SIGNIFICANT HOUSING PROJECTS PLANNED FOR MY
DISTRICT.\r\n\r\nTHE QUESTIONS ECHOING IN OUR COMMUNITY ARE CRITICAL, AND VALID. CAN
OUR INFRASTRUCTURE SUPPORT FUTURE GROWTH? DOES MAUI HAVE THE CARRYING-CAPACITY FOR
THIS PACE OF CONSTRUCTION? AND IS ADDING MORE HOUSING UNITS TRULY SUSTAINABLE FOR
OUR ISLAND'S FUTURE? THIS MORNING, I WANT TO ADDRESS THESE CONCERNS DIRECTLY. I
BELIEVE THAT BY UNDERSTANDING THE NUANCES OF SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT, AND BY DRAWING
LESSONS FROM MY OWN LIFE EXPERIENCES, WE CAN SEE THAT THESE CHALLENGES ALSO PREVENT
-- PRESENT SIGNIFICANT OPPORTUNITIES FOR A STRONGER, MORE RESILIENT MAUI COUNTY. MY
POLICY POSITIONS PARTICULARLY MY SUPPORT OF COMMUNITY HOUSING DEVELOPMENT, HAVE
SOMETIMES BEEN QUESTIONED.\r\n\r\nTO UNDERSTAND MY APPROACH, IT'S IMPORTANT TO
UNDERSTAND THE VALUES THAT HAVE SHAPED ME. SO IF YOU WILL INDULGE ME FOR A MOMENT. I
