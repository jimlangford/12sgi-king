Built for many — onboard a new tenant

Every government, every artist, every mission is a tenant.

Each tenant is tracked separately — its own data, its own reports, its own surfaces — so the work never bleeds between them. Onboarding a new county, a new state, or a new creator is the same repeatable step: register the tenant, point it at its sources, and its pages fill themselves.
