govOS — one charter, sixteen governments, up to the Holy See

The 12 Stones Sovereign Charter crosswalked against the real law and the real money of sixteen governments — Hawaiʻi (State + 4 counties), New York (State, NYC, a village), the Holy See, and eight world financial capitals. Every figure traces to a public record; every correlation is a question, never an accusation. Act local, think global.

Naga — the name of this unified system — honors the moʻo lineage: the Sanskrit nāga, the serpent-dragon guardian of water, carried across the ocean into ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi as moʻo, the Hawaiian dragon-lizard kiaʻi (guardian) of fresh water and sacred places. One guardian of the source, many waters.
