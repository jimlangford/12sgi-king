The reusable substrate for the map-forward civic tenant. Tokens are verbatim from the 12sgi-design canon; the verdict tokens (--served-people / --served-money / --record-gap) are the PROPOSAL layer, each anchored to a canon token — served-people = --ok, served-money = --accent gold, record-gap = --info dusty blue (honest-empty, deliberately NOT error-red — a gap is not wrongdoing). Warm-dark govOS register, one gold accent, JetBrains Mono on every machine value, emoji glyphs only.
