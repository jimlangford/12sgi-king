Who Funds Our Elections

Campaign money is public record and lawful to give. This page sorts every reported contribution by the giver's own stated industry — so you can see which sectors spend the most to shape who governs, who their biggest givers are, and which candidates they back. Real estate and development are broken out first, because that is where the housing stakes live.

Classification is a transparent keyword heuristic on self-reported employer and occupation from the filings — a map to read the record, not a legal judgment. "Other / Unclassified" holds blanks, "self / retired," and givers whose reported field matched no class. The question is always whether the votes and contracts follow the money — asked, never asserted.
