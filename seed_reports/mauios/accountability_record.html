Why Kilo Aupuni watches what it watches

  • Stant stole through SOLE-SOURCE procurement -> that is exactly why Kilo Aupuni's bids-watch tracks every county contract and flags sole-source awards.
  • The reform that matters most -- RECUSAL over disclosure -- is exactly what the officials-scorecard tracks from the minutes.
  • Bribes followed the MONEY into contracts and campaigns -> donor-watch and patterns join campaign money to votes and contracts.
  • The lawful path is already on the books: HRS 84 (ethics), HRS 11 (campaign finance), HRS 103D (procurement), HRS 92/92F (Sunshine/UIPA), federal honest-services fraud.
