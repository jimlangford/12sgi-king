Paris elections
Source: CNCCFP (cnccfp.fr). France has mandatory campaign finance disclosure since 1988. Campaign accounts for presidential, legislative, and municipal elections are audited by the CNCCFP. Accounts are published in the Journal officiel and searchable at cnccfp.fr. Donations from legal entities (companies) to political parties are PROHIBITED under French law — only individuals may donate.

Anne Hidalgo (PS/Socialist Party) was re-elected Mayor of Paris in June 2020. Municipal election campaign accounts are filed with the CNCCFP within 2 months of the election. The 2020 accounts are publicly available in the Journal officiel de la République française.
