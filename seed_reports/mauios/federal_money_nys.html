Medicaid — dominant federal stream

HHS / CMS — Medicaid FMAP ~$40B+/yr
Federal Medicaid matching to New York State. New York has approximately 7.3 million Medicaid enrollees (one of the largest programs in the US). The federal government matches NYS Medicaid spending at approximately 50% FMAP. Total NYS Medicaid spending is approximately $80B/year; the federal match is approximately $40B+. Federal Medicaid funds flow through the State Division of Health (DOH) and are tracked in the NYS Executive Budget. This is by far the largest federal transfer to New York.
Source: NYS Executive Budget ↗ · CMS FMAP rates (cms.gov)

Infrastructure — IIJA (Bipartisan Infrastructure Law)

DOT / FHWA / FTA / EPA / others $14B+ over 5 years
Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA, 2021) — NYS allocations. New York is among the top recipients of IIJA infrastructure funding: (1) FHWA highway formula: ~$1.8B/year for roads and bridges; (2) FTA formula grants for MTA, LIRR, Metro-North: ~$2B+/year; (3) EPA Clean Water/Drinking Water State Revolving Funds: ~$700M/year; (4) Broadband deployment (NTIA): ~$1.1B total; (5) EV infrastructure: ~$175M. Full NYS IIJA breakdown at whitehouse.gov/build/locations/new-york and budget.ny.gov.
Source: whitehouse.gov/invest/ny ↗ · NYS DOT

Education

DOE — Title I, IDEA, ESSER $3B+/yr
Federal education grants to New York State Department of Education. NYS receives approximately $3B+ annually in federal education grants: (1) Title I (low-income students): ~$1B/yr; (2) IDEA (students with disabilities): ~$600M/yr; (3) Title II, III, IV (teachers, English learners, well-rounded ed): ~$400M/yr. COVID-era ESSER funds totaled approximately $9B to NYS schools over 2020–2024. NYSED publishes allocation data at nysed.gov.
Source: NYSED federal grants ↗ · ED ESSA data explorer

Housing — HUD programs

HUD — CDBG + HOME + Section 8 $2B+/yr (state share)
HUD formula grants to NYS outside NYC. NYS HTFC (Housing Trust Fund Corporation) and county governments receive: CDBG entitlement and state program funds (~$200M/yr), HOME Investment Partnerships (~$100M/yr), and Housing Choice Voucher (Section 8) program funding (~$1B+ state-administered outside NYC). The NYS HTFC administers HUD competitive grants for affordable housing development. Full program data at hcr.ny.gov.
Source: NYS HCR ↗ · HUD IDIS database

ARPA / American Rescue Plan

Treasury — ARPA SLFRF ~$12.7B (state + localities)
American Rescue Plan Act — State and Local Fiscal Recovery Funds to NYS. New York State received approximately $12.7B in SLFRF: ~$8.9B direct to the state government, and ~$3.8B to localities outside NYC. NYS allocated ARPA funds to: COVID response, healthcare workforce, broadband, water infrastructure, housing, small business, and fiscal relief. NYS ARPA tracker at budget.ny.gov. Federal recipient data at USASpending.gov.
Source: Treasury SLFRF ↗ · NYS Budget ↗
